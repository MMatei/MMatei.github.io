Invisible

Effects. Cannot be seen except by magical means, though the presence of the creature may be guessed from the sounds it makes or footprints that it leaves behind. Unless an enemy has a specific way of pinpointing the creature, it has disadvantage attacking its Deflection, and the creature can attack vs Deflection with advantage.
Removal. Depends on the conditions of invisibility. Some abilities or spells allow a creature to see invisible creatures.
