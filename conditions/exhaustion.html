Exhaustion

Effects. Exhaustion is measured in six levels of increasing gravity. A creature suffers the effects of its current level of exhaustion as well as the effect of the lower levels. The exhaustion levels are:
  1. Disadvantage on ability checks.
  2. Speed halved.
  3. Disadvantage on attack rolls and saves.
  4. Max hp halved.
  5. Speed reduced to 0.
  6. Death.
Removal. A long rest reduces exhaustion by one level, provided that the character has had sufficient food and drink. Other effects may also reduce exhaustion by a specified number of levels.
