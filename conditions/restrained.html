Restrained

Effects. The creature:
  • cannot move and cannot benefit from bonuses to speed
  • has disadvantage on attack rolls and Dexterity saves
  • attacks against it have advantage
Removal. Depends on what is restraining the creature, but generally the creature can break free with a Str check, or can be helped out by an ally. It might also be possible to wiggle free with a Dex check, or to cut the restraints with a melee weapon attack.
