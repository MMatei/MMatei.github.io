Restrained

Effects. The creature:
  • cannot move and cannot benefit from bonuses to speed
  • has disadvantage on attacks vs Deflection
  • attacks vs its Deflection or Reflex have advantage
Removal. Depends on what is restraining the creature, but generally the creature can break free with a Strength (Athletics) / Dexterity (Escape Artist) check, or can be helped out by an ally with a similar check. It might also be possible to cut the restraints with a melee weapon attack.
