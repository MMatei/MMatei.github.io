Blinded

Effects. The creature:
  • can't see, so it automatically fails all checks requiring sight
  • can't take the Disengage action
  • moves at half speed
  • has disadvantage on attacking Deflection
  • attacks on its Deflection, Reflex have advantage
Removal. The creature's sight must be restored; that may be as simple as removing a blindfold, or it might require lesser restoration, for magical blindness. If the creature's eyes have been put out, then only regenerate can grow them back and remove blindness (or perhaps some magical eyes...).
