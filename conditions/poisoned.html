Poisoned

Effects. The creature has disadvantage on all rolls. If the creature reaches 0 hp while poisoned, it becomes paralyzed for an hour even if it regains hp.
If a creature has become intoxicated with alcohol or some drug with similar effects, consider the creature to be poisoned.
Removal. Lesser restoration or more powerful magic, or an appropriate potion. If 'poisoned' with alcohol, then sufficient rest will remove the condition.
