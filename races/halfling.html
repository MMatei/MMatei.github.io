Halfling

Lore

[History 30] With the resounding success of gnomes as mining slaves, the rulers of the Eternal Empire soon decided that it would be very much worth it to engineer a docile race especially for agricultural production. The result of this effort was halflings. Even after the Empire fell, and thousands of years elapsed, not all halflings were released from their servitude, for the humans appreciated the great financial advantages these slaves presented.

Halflings are optimistic creatures, who try to make the best of their circumstances. They are very kind-hearted and emotional, prone to great bursts of joy or tears. They are very pacifistic, as it is against their very being to hurt another living being. Back them into a corner, however, and halflings can demonstrate great bravery and camaraderie. They are the least adventurous of all races, prefering the comforts of hearth and home to the unknowns of the road.

Halflings like living in rural settings and few move on to cities. They can make very good traders, however, for they are thrifty and good stewards, and those halflings adventurous enough to travel have amassed great wealth from commerce. Halflings don't like to expose their wealth, however, not only because they are content with simple things, but also because they are loathe to attract unwanted attention. Halflings want to blend in with the other races in their communities, and they try to attract as little attention as possible.

While there are regions populated solely by halflings, they have no political entities of their own. Halflings have almost zero interest in politics or power and prefer to let others rule over them. It is indeed very hard to get a halfling population to revolt, at least as long as their food supply is not threatened - halflings are very fond of their meals.


Traits

Ability Score Increase. +1 Intuition.

Age. Halflings mature at the same rate as humans and live until 100-120 years. Because of their quiet lifestyle, many more halflings live to see old age.

Alignment. Halflings are pretty firmly good. Because of their heightened emotional sensitivity, they can't really bear to be selfish. They aren't very lawful, however, as they appreciate their freedom.

Size. Halflings are 1.2 to 1.4 meters tall, slightly smaller than dwarves. They are, however, significantly less bulky, weighing 40-60 kg. Their size is Small.

Speed. Your base walking speed is 6 squares. Despite their short legs, halflings are nimble and can get around surprisingly fast.

Lucky. When you roll a 1 on any roll, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll.

Brave. Rolls that attempt to frighten you are made with disadvantage.

Halfling Nimbleness. You can move through the space of any creature that is of a size larger than yours.

Naturally Stealthy. You can attempt to hide even when you are obscured only by a creature that is at least one size larger than you.

Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common. Halflings rarely study languages from outside their community.
