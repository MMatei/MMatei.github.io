Kenku

Lore

The Kenku are the favored children of the trickster God Loki, evolved from birds as the perfect manipulators, forgers and tricksters.


Traits

Ability Score Increase. +1 Memory.

Age. Kenku burn bright, but burn fast, reaching maturity around 12 years and living to around 60.

Alignment. Kenku love freedom, and can be fairly selfish.

Size. Kenku are of slender build, but shorter than humans, at around 1.3 to 1.6 meters tall. They weigh between 75-95 kg. Their size is Medium.

Speed. Your base walking speed is 6 squares.

Mimicry. You can mimic any sound that you have heard clearly and that you remember. If imitating a complex sound, like someone's voice, you must roll proficiency + Memory vs INT against all creatures you wish to fool.

Expert Forgery. You can duplicate other creatures' handwriting and craftwork. You have advantage on all checks made to produce forgeries or duplicates of existing objects.

Limited Flight. A kenku's wings are not powerful enough to enable it to soar into the air, but they can be used effectively to jump for twice the height or width normally possible and to slow down descent so that the kenku never takes falling damage while conscious.

Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common and Auran. Auran is a language of whistles that has no official written form.
