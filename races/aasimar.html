Aasimar

Lore

The aasimar (also called high men) are the children of celestials and humans, born with a heightened sense of justice and righteousness. They are very pious, however, and they see law as a matter of faith, not reason, which leads them to be often inflexible in their outlook. Other races often find them to be too rigid and far too eager to view everything in stark, black or white terms. In fact, some aasimar may have shock when forced with the hard and unpleasant choices inherent in life. Many an aasimar has been driven to suicide rather than be forced to compromise his/her beliefs.

Religion is extremely important to the aasimar, and they devote at least an hour every day for prayer and honoring of the Deities. Their settlements are filled with temples to all Deities, grand and small. In fact, aasimar can be even rather superstitious, sicne they are so focused on never upsetting any of the 'good' Deities.


Traits

Ability Score Increase. +1 Intuition.

Age. Aasimar mature at the same rate humans do and reach adulthood around the age of 20. They live somewhat longer than humans, however, often exceeding 150 years.

Alignment. Due to their celestial ancestry, aasimar are strongly inclined towards lawful alignments and are generally selfless.

Size. Similar size and build to humans, from 1.6 to 1.8 meters tall, on occasion even taller. Their size is Medium.

Speed. Your base walking speed is 6 squares.

Light Bearer. You can cast the light cantrip at will.

Healing Touch. As an action, you can touch a creature and cause it to regain a number of hit points equal to your level. Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest.

Radiant Soul. Starting at 3rd level, you can use your action to unleash the divine energy within yourself, causing your eyes to glimmer and two luminous, incorporeal wings to sprout from your back. Your transformation lasts for 1 minute or until you end it as a bonus action. During it, you have a flying speed of 6 squares, and once on each of your turns, you can deal extra force damage to one target when you deal damage to it with an attack or a spell. The extra force damage equals your level.

Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest.

Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common and one more language of your choice.
