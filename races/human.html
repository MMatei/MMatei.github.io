Human

Lore

Humans are the most widespread and diverse race in the world of Neveria, and the most willing to explore, colonize and live alongside other races (and also interbreed with them - humans rarely shy away from breeding!). They live in almost every habitat, which has led to incredible diversity among them, in skin color, eye shape, hair types, and even height. Their unbounded curiosity and ambition has led them to forge countless varied nations, all of whom inevitably crumble within centuries, however, because of human short-sightedness and hubris.

Thanks to their adaptability, humans find it easy to live among different cultures, and human nations have often eagerly embraced different races. But as a race of extremes, humans can sometimes also fall into xenophobia, and conduct vicious single-minded purges of other races (at times even other humans).

Humans are the creation of Darun-Nurad, the God of Balance, who wanted his children to be capable of experiencing the whole gamut of emotions in the world. Their choices would be meaningless, after all, if they weren't equally disposed towards all of them.

Traits

Attribute Increase. +1 for three attributess of your choice. You can choose to forego one of these attribute increases in exchange for a feat of your choice.

Age. Humans reach adulthood in their late teens and usually live for less than a century.

Alignment. Humans tend toward no particular alignment. The best and the worst are found among them.

Size. Humans vary quite wildy in height and build, from 1.5 to 1.8 meters tall, on rare occasions even taller. Their size is Medium.

Speed. Your base walking speed is 6 squares.

Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common and one extra language of your choice. Humans typically learn the languages of other peoples they deal with, including obscure dialects. They are fond of sprinkling their speech with words borrowed from other tongues: Orc curses, Elvish musical expressions, Dwarvish military phrases, and so on.
