Boiling Rays

Level II Protomancy
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Things within 24 squares (36m)
Effect: You create three rays of concentrated heat and aim them at targets of your choice (you can aim all three at the same target). Roll spellpower vs Deflection for each ray. On hit, deal 2d6 fire damage.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you create one additional ray for each slot level above 2nd.

Light orange rays spring from your hand, and any creature hit is boiled alive.
