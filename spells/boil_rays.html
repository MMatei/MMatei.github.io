Boiling Rays

Classes. Sorcerer Wizard
School. Level 2 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 24 squares (36m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous

You create three rays of concentrated heat and aim them at targets within range. You can target each ray on a separate creature. Make a ranged spell attack for each ray. On a hit, the target takes 2d6 fire damage.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you create one additional ray for each slot level above 2nd.
