Boiling Rays

Level II Protomancy
Classes. Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 24 squares (36m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. You create three rays of concentrated heat and aim them at targets within range. You can target each ray on a separate creature. Roll spellpower vs AC for each ray. On a hit, the target takes 2d6 fire damage.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you create one additional ray for each slot level above 2nd.

Light orange rays spring from your hand, and any creature hit is boiled alive.
