Mending

Level I Transmutation
Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Wizard
Casting Time: 10 rounds
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Touch
Effect: This spell repairs a single break or tear, along with minor scratches and bent metal, in an object you touch, such as a mangled key, a torn cloak, or a leaking waterskin. As long as the break or tear is no longer than 50cm in any dimension, you mend it, leaving no trace of the former damage. You cannot use this spell to join together two objects that were once whole, like two sword pieces.
