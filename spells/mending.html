Mending

Classes. Bard Cleric Druid Sorcerer Wizard
School. Transmutation Cantrip
Casting Time. 10 rounds
Range. Touch
Components. V, S, M (two lodestones)
Duration. Instantaneous

This spell repairs a single break or tear in an object you touch, such as a broken key, a torn cloak, or a leaking wineskin. As long as the break or tear is no longer than 50cm in any dimension, you mend it, leaving no trace of the former damage.
