Immolation

Level V Protomancy
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: A creature within 18 squares (27m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Warding. On hit, the target takes 8d6 fire damage and is set on fire for 10 rounds. You must repeat the roll at the end of each of the target's turns in order to maintain the effect. On miss, the target only takes half damage. The burning target sheds bright light in a 6 square radius and dim light for an additional 6 squares. The damage it takes from being on fire is increased to 2d6 fire damage.
