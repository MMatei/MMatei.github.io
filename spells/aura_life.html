Aura of Life

Classes. Paladin
School. Level 4 Abjuration
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self (6 square radius)
Components. V
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Life-preserving energy radiates from you in an aura with a 6-square (9m) radius. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. Each nonhostile creature in the aura (including you) has resistance to necrotic damage, and its hit point maximum can't be reduced. In addition, a nonhostile, living creature regains 1 hp when it starts its turn in the aura with 0 hp.
