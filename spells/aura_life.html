Aura of Life

Level IV Abjuration
Classes: Paladin
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Target: Self (6 square radius aura)
Effect: Life-preserving energy radiates from you in an aura with a 6-square (9m) radius. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. Each nonhostile creature in the aura (including you) has resistance to necrotic damage, and its hit point maximum can't be reduced. In addition, a nonhostile, living creature regains 1 hp when it starts its turn in the aura with 0 hp.
