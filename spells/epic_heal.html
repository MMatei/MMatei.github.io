Wellspring of Life

Level IX Animancy
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Every creature of your choice you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: You restore up to 700 hp, divided as you choose among the targets. Creatures healed by this spell are also cured of all diseases and any effect making them blinded or deafened. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.
