Gust

Transmutation Cantrip
Classes. Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. You seize the air and compel it to create one of the following effects at a point you can see within range:
  • Roll spellpower vs STR against one Medium or smaller creature in range; on hit, it is pushed up to 1 square away from you. If pushed into an immovable obstacle, the creature takes 1d4 crushing damage.
  • You create a small blast of air capable of moving one object that is neither held nor carried and that weighs no more than 2kg. The object is pushed up to 2 squares away from you. It isn't pushed with enough force to cause damage.
  • You create a harmless sensory affect using air, such as causing leaves to rustle, wind to slam shutters shut, or your clothing to ripple in a breeze.
