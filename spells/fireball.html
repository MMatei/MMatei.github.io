Fireball

Level III Protomancy
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: All creatures in a 4-square radius sphere centered on a point within 30 squares (45m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, a target takes 8d6 fire damage, or half damage otherwise.
The fire spreads around corners. An easily flammable object hit by this spell ignites if it isn't being worn or carried by a creature who spends its reaction to put out the fire.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.

A bright streak flashes from your pointing finger to a point you choose within range and then blossoms with a low roar into an explosion of flame.
