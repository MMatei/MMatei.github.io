Fireball

Classes. Sorcerer Wizard
School. Level 3 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 30 squares (45m)
Components. V, S, M (a tiny ball of bat guano and sulfur)
Duration. Instantaneous

A bright streak flashes from your pointing finger to a point you choose within range and then blossoms with a low roar into an explosion of flame. Each creature in a 4-square-radius sphere centered on that point must make a Dexterity saving throw. A target takes 8d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
