Fireball

Level III Protomancy
Classes. Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 30 squares (45m)
Material Components. —
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Roll spellpower vs DEX against every creature in a 4-square-radius sphere centered on a point within range. On hit, a target takes 8d6 fire damage, or half as much damage on a failure.
The fire spreads around corners. It ignites flammable objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 2d6 for each slot level above 3rd.

A bright streak flashes from your pointing finger to a point you choose within range and then blossoms with a low roar into an explosion of flame.
