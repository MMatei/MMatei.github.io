Terror

Level IX Umbramancy
Classes: Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: All creatures in a 6-square sphere centered on a point within 24 squares (36m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Intuition against afflicted creatures. On hit, the creature takes 4d10 psychic damage, drops whatever it is holding, and becomes paralyzed in abject terror for the duration. On miss, the the creature only takes half damage.
At the start of subsequent turns, repeat the spellpower vs Intuition roll. On hit, the creature takes 4d10 psychic damage. On miss, the spell ends.

Dark clouds of Shadow gather around the eyes of all creatures in the area of effect, making them see naught but a suffocating darkness that holds them in place and threatens to choke them.
