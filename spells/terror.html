Terror

Classes. Wizard
School. Level 9 Illusion
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 24 squares (36m)
Area of effect. 6 square (6m) sphere centered on a point within range
Components. V, S
Duration. Up to 10 rounds

Dark clouds of Shadow gather around the eyes of all creatures in the area of effect, making them see naught but a suffocating darkness that holds them in place and threatens to choke them. An afflicted creature must make an Intuition save or drop whatever it is holding and become paralyzed in abject terror for the duration.
At the end of their turns, affected creatures repeat the Intuition save. If it fails, the creature takes 5d10 psychic damage. If it succeeds, the spell ends.
