Terror

Classes. Wizard
School. Level 3 Illusion
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 24 squares (36m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Up to 10 rounds

You infect the minds of every creature in a 6-square sphere centered on a point within range with the most horrible of terrors. An afflicted creature must make a Wisdom save or drop whatever it is holding and become paralyzed in abject terror for the duration.
At the end of their turns, affected creatures repeat the Wisdom save. If it fails, the creature takes 5d10 psychic damage. If it succeeds, the spell ends.
