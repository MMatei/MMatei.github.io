Terror

Level IX Umbramancy
Classes: Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 24 squares (36m)
Area of effect: 6 square (6m) sphere centered on a point within range
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect: Dark clouds of Shadow gather around the eyes of all creatures in the area of effect, making them see naught but a suffocating darkness that holds them in place and threatens to choke them. Roll spellpower vs INT against afflicted creatures. On hit, the creature takes 4d10 psychic damage, drops whatever it is holding, and becomes paralyzed in abject terror for the duration. On miss, the the creature takes half damage and suffers no ill effects.
At the start of subsequent turns, repeat the spellpower vs INT roll. On hit, the creature takes 4d10 psychic damage. On miss, the spell ends.
