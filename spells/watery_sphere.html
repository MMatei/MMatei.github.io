Watery Sphere

Level IV Conjuration
Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Range: 18 squares (27m)
Effect: You conjure up a sphere of water with a 2-square radius on a point you can see within range. The sphere can hover in the air, but no more than 3m off the ground. The sphere remains for the spellâ€™s duration.
Roll spellpower vs Firmness against every creature in the sphere's space. On hit, a creature is restrained by the sphere and is engulfed by the water. At the end of each of its turns, you must repeat the roll to keep the target restrained. On miss, a target is ejected from that space to the nearest unoccupied space outside it. You automatically miss Huge or larger creatures.
The sphere can restrain a maximum of four Medium or smaller creatures or one Large creature. If the sphere restrains a creature in excess of these numbers, a random creature that was already restrained by the sphere falls out of it and lands prone in a space within 1 square of it.
As an action, you can move the sphere up to 6 squares in a straight line. If it moves over a pit, cliff, or other drop, it safely descends until it is hovering 3m over ground. Any creature restrained by the sphere moves with it. You can ram the sphere into a creature, thus making the roll to restrain it, but no more than once per turn.
When the spell ends, the sphere falls to the ground and extinguishes all normal flames within 6 squares of it. Any creature restrained by the sphere is knocked prone in the space where it falls.
