Poison Spray

< Conjuration Cantrip
Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: A creature you can see within 3 squares (4.5m)
Effects: Roll spellpower vs Endurance. On hit, deal 1d12 poison damage.
At Higher Levels: This spell's damage is 2d12 for a caster of level 5, 3d12 for a caster of level 11, 4d12 for a caster of level 17 and 5d12 for a caster of level 23.

You extend your hand towards your target and a spray of venomous-green liquid shoots from your hand, enveloping the victim, worming its way inside through every open pore, killing them from within.
