Create Bonfire

Protomancy Cantrip
Classes. Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 12 squares (18m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. You start a fire in a square you can see within range. Any creature in that square, that enters it, or that ends its turn there, must succeed on a Dexterity save or take 1d10 fire damage. A creature can only be damaged by this spell once per round.
At Higher Levels. This spell's damage is 2d10 for a caster of level 5, 3d10 for a caster of level 11, 4d10 for a caster of level 17 and 5d10 for a caster of level 23.

You start a great fire from nothing, with heat enough to roast a whole cow. Let's see them try to pass through there without getting burnt!
