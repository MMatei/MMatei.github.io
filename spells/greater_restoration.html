Greater Restoration

Level V Abjuration
Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: Emerald dust worth at least 100 gg, which the spell consumes
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Self, or a creature within 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: You can reduce the target’s exhaustion level by one, or end one of the following effects on the target:
  • One effect that charmed or petrified the target.
  • One curse, including the target’s attunement to a cursed magic item.
  • Any reduction to one of the target’s ability scores.
  • One effect reducing the target’s hit point maximum.
