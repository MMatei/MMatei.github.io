Hellish Rebuke

Level I Protomancy
Classes. Warlock
Casting Time. 1 reaction, which you take in response to being damaged by a creature within 12 squares (18m) of you that you can see.
Range. 12 squares (18m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Roll spellpower vs DEX against the creature that damaged you. On hit, it takes 2d10 fire damage, or half as much on failure.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 1st.

You point your finger, and the creature that dared harm you is momentarily surrounded by hellish flames.
