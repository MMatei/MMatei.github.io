Ray of Sickness

Level I Animancy
Classes. Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 12 squares (18m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Roll spellpower vs CON. On hit, the target takes 2d8 poison damage and is poisoned until the end of your next turn. On failure, the target only takes half damage and is not poisoned.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

A ray of sickening greenish energy lashes out towards your foe, necrotic energy refined into a dangerous magical poison.
