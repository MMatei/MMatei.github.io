Destructive Wave

Level V Protomancy
Classes: Paladin
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Every creature you choose within 6 squares of you
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Endurance. On hit, a target takes 10d6 sonic damage and is knocked prone. On miss, only deal half damage.

Empowered with divine might, you strike the ground, sending a mighty shockwave that leaves friends unharmed and knocks foes to the ground.
