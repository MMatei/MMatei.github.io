Shocking Grasp

Protomancy Cantrip
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: A creature within 1 square of you
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Deflection. You have advantage on the attack roll if the target is wearing armor made of metal or if it is wet. On hit, the target takes 1d10 electric damage, and it can't take reactions until the start of its next turn.
At Higher Levels: This spell's damage is 2d10 for a caster of level 5, 3d10 for a caster of level 11, 4d10 for a caster of level 17 and 5d10 for a caster of level 23.

A powerful jolt travels from your hand into the target you touched, delivering a stunning shock that leaves the target unable to react in a timely fashion to its surroundings.
