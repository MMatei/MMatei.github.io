Darkness

Level II Umbramancy
Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour
Target: 4-square radius sphere centered on a point within 24 squares (36m)
Effect: Magical darkness covers the target area for the duration, spreading around corners. A creature with darkvision can’t see through this darkness, and nonmagical light can’t illuminate it. If the point you choose is on an object you are holding or one that isn’t being worn or carried, the darkness emanates from the object and moves with it. Completely covering the source of the darkness with an opaque object, such as a bowl or a helm, blocks the darkness. If any of this spell’s area overlaps with an area of light created by a spell of 2nd level or lower, the spell that created the light is dispelled.
