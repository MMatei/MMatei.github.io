Shatter

Level II Protomancy
Classes. Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 12 squares (18m)
Components. V, S, M (a chip of stone)
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Roll spellpower vs CON against every creature in a 2-square-radius sphere centered on a point of your choice within range. You have advantage against creatures made of inorganic material (stone, crystal, metal, etc). On hit, a creature takes 3d8 thunder damage, or half as much damage on failure.
A nonmagical object that isn’t being worn or carried also takes the damage if it’s in the spell’s area.
The spell makes a very loud noise that can be heard up to 90m away.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd. A sudden loud ringing noise, painfully intense, erupts from a point of your choosing. Sonic waves ripple out from it and threaten to shatter all inorganic materials. Organics aren't much safer either, as the violent waves turn their innards into mush.
