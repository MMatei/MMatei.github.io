Elemental Weapon

Classes. Paladin
School. Level 3 Transmutation
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. Touch
Components. V, S
Duration. Concentration, up to 1 hour

A weapon you touch becomes a magic weapon (if it wasn't already). Choose one of the following damage types: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder. For the duration, the weapon has a +1 bonus to attack rolls and deals an extra 1d6 damage of the chosen type when it hits.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the attack bonus increases by +1 and the extra damage by 1d6 for every level above 4th.
