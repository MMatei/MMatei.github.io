Scorching Line

Level II Protomancy
Classes. Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. V, S, M (a red dragon's scale)
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. A line of roaring flame 6 squares long and 1 square wide emanates from you in a direction you choose. Roll spellpower vs DEX against every creature in the line. On hit, a creature takes 4d8 fire damage, or half as much damage on failure.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
