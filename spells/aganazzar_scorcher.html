Aganazzar's Scorcher

Classes. Sorcerer Wizard
School. Level 2 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. V, S, M (a red dragon's scale)
Duration. Instantaneous

A line of roaring flame 6 squares long and 1 square wide emanates from you in a direction you choose. Each creature in the line must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 3d8 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
