Flesh to Stone

Level VI Transmutation
Classes: Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: A creature made of flesh that you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Endurance. On hit, the target is restrained as its flesh begins to harden. Then, at the end of each of its turns, roll spellpower vs Endurance. If you successfully hit three times, the target is turned to stone and becomes petrified for the duration. If you miss three times, however, the spell ends.
