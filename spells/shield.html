Shield

Level I Abjuration
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you are hit by an attack or targeted by the magic missile spell
Material Components: —
Duration: 1 round
Target: Self
Effect: An invisible barrier of magical force appears and protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to Deflection, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from magic missile.
