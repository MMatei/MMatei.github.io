Stoneskin

Level IV Abjuration
Classes: Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour
Target: Self, or a willing creature within 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: Until the spell ends, the target has resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.

The target's skin becomes hard as stone and cold to the touch.
