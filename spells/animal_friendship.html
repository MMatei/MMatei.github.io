Animal Friendship

Level I Noumancy
Classes. Bard, Druid, Ranger
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. V, S, M (a morsel of food)
Duration. 24 hours
Effect. Choose a beast that you can see within range. It must see and hear you. If the beastâ€™s Intelligence modifier is -2 or higher, the spell fails. Otherwise, the beast must succeed on an Intuition save or be charmed by you for the spell's duration. If you or one of your companions harms the target, the spell ends.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can affect one additional beast for each slot level above 1st.

With an offering of food and a gentle prod on the mind, you try to convince a beast that you mean it no harm. Highly intelligent animals cannot be convinced with this crude method, however.
