Animal Friendship

Classes. Bard Druid Ranger
School. Level 1 Enchantment
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. V, S, M (a morsel of food)
Duration. 24 hours

This spell lets you convince a beast that you mean it no harm. Choose a beast that you can see within range. It must see and hear you. If the beast’s Intelligence modifier is -3 or higher, the spell fails. Otherwise, the beast must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be charmed by you for the spell’s duration. If you or one of your companions harms the target, the spell ends.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can affect one additional beast for each slot level above 1st.
