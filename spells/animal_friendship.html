Animal Friendship

Level I Noumancy
Classes: Bard, Druid, Ranger
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 24 hours
Target: A beast with instinctive intelligence that can see and hear you with 6 squares (9m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Will. On hit, the beast is charmed by you for the spell's duration. If you or one of your companions harms the target, the spell ends.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can affect one additional beast for each slot level above 1st. Instead of targeting two instinctive beasts, you can cast the spell on one clever beast.
