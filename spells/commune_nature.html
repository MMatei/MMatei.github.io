Commune with Nature

Classes. Druid Ranger
School. Level 5 Divination Ritual
Casting Time. 10 rounds
Range. Self
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous

You briefly become one with nature and gain knowledge of the surrounding territory. In the outdoors, the spell gives you knowledge of the land within 3km of you. In caves and other natural underground settings, the radius is limited to 300m. The spell doesnâ€™t function where nature has been replaced by construction, such as in dungeons and towns.

  • powerful celestials, fey, fiends, elementals, or undead
  • influence from other planes of existence
