Wrathful Smite

Level I Noumancy
Classes. Paladin
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. Self
Components. V
Duration. Up to 10 rounds
Effect. The next time you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack during this spell's duration, your attack deals an extra 1d6 psychic damage and the spell ends. Additionally, the target must make an Intuition save or be frightened of you for 4 rounds. As an action, the creature can make an Intuition check against your spell save DC to steel its resolve and end this spell.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

You pour your scorn for your enemy into your blade, and it gains a slight purple hue. Any wound made with this weapon will now also damage the mind.
