Eldritch Tendrils

Level I Conjuration
Classes. Warlock
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self (2-square radius)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Roll spellpower vs STR against every creature within 2 squares of you. On hit, a target takes 2d6 force damage and can't take reactions until its next turn. On failure, the creature takes half damage, but suffers no other effect.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

You flirt with insanity as you invoke the power of horrors beyond this dimension. Unnatural tentacles erupt from you, blacker than sin and absorbing all light that falls upon them. Constantly writhing, they grasp at all other creatures nearby, destroying their very life force with their darkness.
