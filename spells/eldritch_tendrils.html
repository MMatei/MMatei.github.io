Eldritch Tendrils

Classes. Warlock
School. Level 1 Conjuration
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self (2-square radius)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous

You flirt with insanity as you invoke the power of horrors beyond this dimension. Tendrils of dark energy erupt from you and batter all creatures within 2 squares of you. Each creature in that area must make a Strength saving throw. On a failed save, a target takes 2d6 necrotic damage and can't take reactions until its next turn. On a successful save, the creature takes half damage, but suffers no other effect.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
