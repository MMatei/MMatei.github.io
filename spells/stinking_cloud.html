Stinking Cloud

Level III Conjuration
Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Range: 18 squares (27m)
Effect: You create a 4-square-radius sphere of yellow, nauseating gas centered on a point within range. The cloud spreads around corners, and its area is lightly obscured. The cloud lingers in the air for the duration, unless dispersed by strong wind.
Roll spellpower vs Endurance against any creature that is completely within the cloud at the start of its turn. On hit, the creature spends its action that turn retching and reeling, and it becomes poisoned until it spends an entire round outside the cloud.
