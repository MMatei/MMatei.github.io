Cordon of Arrows

Level II Transmutation
Classes: Ranger
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: Four arrows or crossbow bolts, which the spell consumes
Duration: 8 hours
Range: 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: You plant four arrows or crossbow bolts in the ground within range and lay magic upon them to protect an area. Until the spell ends, whenever a creature other than you comes within 6 squares (9m) of the ammunition for the first time on a turn or ends its turn there, one piece of ammunition flies up to strike it. Roll spellpower vs Deflection to deal 1d6 + 1 piercing damage (considered magical for resistance purposes). The piece of ammunition is then destroyed. The spell ends when no ammunition remains. When you cast this spell, you can designate any number of creatures, and the spell ignores them.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the amount of ammunition that can be affected increases by four for each slot level above 2nd.
