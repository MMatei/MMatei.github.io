Cure Wounds

Level I Animancy
Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Self, or creature within 1 square
Effect: Target regains a number of hp equal to 1d8 + your spellpower. If the target is undead, it instead takes 1d8 + your spellpower force damage. This spell has no effect on constructs.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing (or damage) increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.
