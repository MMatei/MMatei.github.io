Cure Wounds

Classes. Bard Cleric Druid Paladin Ranger
School. Level 1 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Touch
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous

A creature you touch regains a number of hp equal to 1d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier. If the creature is undead, it instead takes 1d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier radiant damage (because you channel positive energy). This spell has no effect on constructs.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing (or damage) increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.
