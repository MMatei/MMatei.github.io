Wall of Fire

Classes. Druid Sorcerer Wizard
School. Level 4 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 24 squares (36m)
Components. V, S, M (a small piece of phosphorus)
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds

You create a wall of fire on a solid surface within range. You can make the wall up to 12 squares long and 6m high, or a ringed wall up to 4 squares in diameter, 6m high. The wall is opaque and lasts for the duration.
When the wall appears, each creature within its area must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 5d8 fire damage, or half as much damage on a successful save.
One side of the wall, selected by you when you cast this spell, deals 5d8 fire damage to each creature that ends its turn within 2 squares of that side or inside the wall. A creature takes the same damage when it enters the wall for the first time on a turn. The other side of the wall deals no damage.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 4th.
