Beacon of Hope

Level III Abjuration
Classes. Cleric
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares
Material Components. —
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. Choose any number of creatures within range. For the duration, each target has advantage on death saves, regains the maximum number of hit points possible from any healing, and attacks against their Will have disadvantage.

Targets are filled with hope and inspiration, which improves healing, as well as their resilience to effects that would sap their will.
