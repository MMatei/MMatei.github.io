Divine Command

Level VII Noumancy
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 quick action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Choose any number of creatures that can hear you within 6 squares (9m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Will. On hit, if the creature has
  • 50 hit points or fewer: blinded for 1 minute
  • 40 hit points or fewer: blinded and deafened for 10 minutes
  • 30 hit points or fewer: blinded, deafened, and stunned for 1 hour
  • 20 hit points or fewer: killed instantly
Regardless of its current hit points, a celestial, an elemental, a fey, or a fiend that is hit is forced back to its plane of origin (if it isn’t there already) and can’t return to your current plane for 24 hours by any means short of a wish spell.

Imbued with authority from your Deity, you can banish any extraplanar, or severely punish other creatures.
