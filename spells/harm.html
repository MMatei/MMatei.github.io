Harm

Level VI Animancy
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous (1 hour side effect)
Target: A creature you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Endurance. On hit, the target takes 14d6 necrotic damage and its max hp is reduced for 1 hour by an amount equal to the necrotic damage it took. On miss, only deal half damage. The damage can’t reduce the target's hp below 1. Any effect that removes a disease allows a creature’s hit point maximum to return to normal before that time passes. This spell has no effect on constructs.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by 2d6 for each slot level above 6th.

A virulent flesh-eating disease spreads and multiplies in the target organism. The only downside... the disease doesn't want to kill its victim!
