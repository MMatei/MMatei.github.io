Dust Devil

Level II Conjuration
Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: An unoccupied 1-square cube of air that you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: An elemental force that resembles a dust devil appears in the cube and lasts for the spellâ€™s duration.
Roll spellpower vs Firmness against any creature that ends its turn within 1 square of the dust devil. On hit, the creature takes 2d8 crushing damage and is pushed prone 1 square away. On miss, the creature takes only half damage.
As a quick action, you can move the dust devil up to 6 squares in any direction. If the dust devil moves over sand, dust, loose dirt, or small gravel, it sucks up the material and forms a 2-square-radius cloud of debris around itself that lasts until the start of your next turn. The cloud heavily obscures its area.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
