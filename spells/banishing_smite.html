Banishing Smite

Level V Abjuration
Classes: Paladin
Casting Time: 1 bonus action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: Self
Effect: The next time you hit a creature with a weapon attack before this spell ends, the attack deals an extra 5d10 force damage to the target. Additionally, if this attack reduces the target to 50 hp or fewer, you banish it. If the target is native to a different plane of existence than the one you're on, the target disappears, returning to its home plane. If the target is native to the plane you're on, the creature vanishes into a harmless demiplane. While there, the target is incapacitated. It remains there until the spell ends, at which point the target reappears in the space it left or in the nearest unoccupied space if that space is occupied.

Your weapon crackles strands of silver that deliver divine judgment to the enemies of the paladin.
