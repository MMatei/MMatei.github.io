Warding Bond

Level II Abjuration
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: A pair of platinum rings worth at least 50 gg each, which you and the target must wear for the duration
Duration: 1 hour
Target: A willing creature you touch
Effect: This spell wards the target and creates a mystic connection between you and the target until the spell ends. While the target is within 12 squares (18m) of you, it gains a +2 bonus to all defenses, and it has resistance to all damage. However, each time it takes damage, you take the same amount of damage.
The spell ends if you drop to 0 hp or if you and the target become separated by more than 12 squares. It also ends if the spell is cast again on either of the connected creatures. You can also dismiss the spell as an action.
