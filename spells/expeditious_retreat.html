Expeditious Retreat

Level I Transmutation
Classes. Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. Self
Components. V, S
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Effect. When you cast this spell, and then as a bonus action on each of your turns until the spell ends, you can take the Dash action.

You strengthen and invigorate your leg muscles, allowing you to keep running far longer than usual.
