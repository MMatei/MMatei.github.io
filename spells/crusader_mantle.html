Crusader's Mantle

Classes. Paladin
School. Level 3 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self
Components. V
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds

Holy power radiates from you in an aura with a 6-square (9m) radius, awakening boldness in friendly creatures. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. While in the aura, each nonhostile creature in the aura (including you) deals an extra 1d4 radiant damage when it hits with a weapon attack.
