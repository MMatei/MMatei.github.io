Crusader's Mantle

Level III Abjuration
Classes. Paladin
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self
Material Components. —
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. Holy power radiates from you in an aura with a 6-square (9m) radius. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. While in the aura, each nonhostile creature in the aura (including you) deals an extra 1d4 force damage when it hits with a weapon attack.

A shining translucent cape forms around the paladin's shoulders. Nearby allies are filled with holy inspiration and vigor, and their blows carry a portion of the paladin's divine might.
