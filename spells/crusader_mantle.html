Crusader's Mantle

Level III Abjuration
Classes. Paladin
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self
Components. V
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. Holy power radiates from you in an aura with a 6-square (9m) radius, awakening boldness in friendly creatures. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. While in the aura, each nonhostile creature in the aura (including you) deals an extra 1d4 force damage when it hits with a weapon attack.
