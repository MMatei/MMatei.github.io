Tenser's Floating Disk

Classes. Wizard
School. Level 1 Conjuration Ritual
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. V, S, M (a drop of mercury)
Duration. 1 hour

This spell creates a circular, horizontal plane of force, 1m in diameter and 2cm thick, that floats 1m above the ground in an unoccupied space of your choice that you can see within range. The disk remains for the duration, and can hold up to 250kg. If more weight is placed on it, the spell ends, and everything on the disk falls to the ground.
The disk is immobile while you are within 4 squares of it. If you move more than 4 squares away from it, the disk follows you so that it remains within 4 squares of you. If can move across uneven terrain, up or down stairs, slopes and the like, but it can’t cross an elevation change of 3m or more. For example, the disk can’t move across a 3m-deep pit, nor could it leave such a pit if it was created at the bottom.
If you move more than 20 squares (30m) away from the disk (typically because it can’t move around an obstacle to follow you), the spell ends.
