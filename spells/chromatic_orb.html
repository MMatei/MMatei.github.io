Chromatic Orb

Classes. Sorcerer Wizard
School. Level 1 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 18 squares (27m)
Components. V, S, M (a diamond worth at least 50gp)
Duration. Instantaneous

You hurl a 10cm-diameter sphere of energy at a creature that you can see within range. You choose acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or thunder for the type of orb you create, and then make a ranged spell attack against the target. If the attack hits, the creature takes 3d8 of the type you chose.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.
