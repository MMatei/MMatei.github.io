Chromatic Orb

Level I Protomancy
Classes. Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 18 squares (27m)
Components. V, S, M (a diamond worth at least 50gp)
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Choose cold, fire, electric, or sonic for the type of orb you create, and then make a ranged spell attack against the target. If the attack hits, the creature takes 3d8 of the type you chose.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

You hurl a 10cm-diameter sphere of energy, which quickly coalesces into the element of your choice: a white swirl of pure frost, an incadescent orb of flame, a mesmerizing tangle of lightning arcs or a weave of concentrated sound.
