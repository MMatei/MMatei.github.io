Searing Smite

Level II Protomancy
Classes. Paladin
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. Self
Components. V
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. The first time you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack during the spell's duration, your weapon flares with white-hot intensity, and the attack deals an extra 2d6 fire damage to the target and causes the target to catch fire until the spell ends. The flame is magical and cannot be extinguished by normal means.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the initial extra damage dealt by the attack increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

You coat your blade in divine fire, which can burn through the most stubborn of materials.
