Geas

Level V Noumancy
Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Wizard
Casting Time: 10 rounds
Material Components: —
Duration: 30 days
Target: A creature you can see within 12 squares (18m), that can understand you
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Intuition. On hit, the target is charmed by you for the duration. While the creature is charmed by you, it takes 4d10 psychic damage each time it acts in a manner directly counter to your instructions. A creature that can't understand you is unaffected by the spell.
You can issue any command you choose, short of an activity that would result in certain death. Should you issue a suicidal command, the spell ends. You can end the spell early by using an action to dismiss it. A remove curse, greater restoration, or wish spell also ends it.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th or 8th level, the duration is 1 year. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 9th level, the spell lasts until it is ended by one of the spells mentioned above.

You bind a creature with a magical command it comes to identify with, to the point that it begins to suffer harrowing depression should it find itself unable to conform to the specified course of action.
