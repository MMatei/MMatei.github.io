Mage Armor

Level I Abjuration
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 8 hours
Target: Self, or a willing creature you touch; must not be wearing any armor
Effect: Until the spell ends, the target's base Deflection becomes 13 + Dexterity. The spell ends if the target dons armor or if you dismiss the spell as an action.

A faintly shimmering field envelops the creature's skin. Any object coming in contact with it is mysteriously slowed, as if cutting through hardened leather.
