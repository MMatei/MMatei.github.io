Mage Armor

Level I Abjuration
Classes. Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Touch
Components. V, S, M (a piece of cured leather)
Duration. 8 hours
Effect. You touch a willing creature who isn't wearing armor. Until the spell ends, the target's base AC becomes 13 + its DEX. The spell ends if the target dons armor or if you dismiss the spell as an action.

A faintly shimmering field envelops the creature's skin. Any object coming in contact with it is mysteriously slowed, as if cutting through hardened leather.
