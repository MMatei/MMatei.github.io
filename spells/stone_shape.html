Stone Shape

Level IV Transmutation
Classes: Cleric, Druid, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: A stone object of Medium size or smaller or a section of stone no more than 1.5m in any dimension that you touch
Effect: You shape stone into any form that suits your purpose. So, for example, you could shape a large rock into a weapon, idol, or coffer, or make a small passage through a wall, as long as the wall is less than 1.5m thick. You could also shape a stone door or its frame to seal the door shut. The object you create can have up to two hinges and a latch, but finer mechanical detail isn't possible.
