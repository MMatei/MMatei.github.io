Silence

Classes. Bard Cleric Ranger
School. Level 2 Illusion Ritual
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 24 squares (36m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 minutes

For the duration, no sound can be created within or pass through a 4-square-radius sphere centered on a point you choose within range. Any creature or object entirely inside the sphere is immune to thunder damage, and creatures are deafened while entirely inside it.
