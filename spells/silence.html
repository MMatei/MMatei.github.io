Silence

Level II Umbramancy Ritual
Classes: Bard, Cleric, Ranger
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Target: A 4-square radius sphere centered on a point within 24 squares (36m)
Effect: For the duration, no sound can be created within or pass through the target area. Any creature or object entirely inside the sphere is immune to thunder damage, and creatures are deafened while entirely inside it.

A favorite spell of last resort for librarians everywhere.
