Circle of Death

Classes. Sorcerer Warlock Wizard
School. Level 6 Necromancy
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 30 squares (45m)
Components. V, S, M (some powder from a crushed black pearl)
Duration. Instantaneous

A sphere of negative energy ripples out in a 12-square radius sphere from a point within range. Each creature in that area must make a Constitution saving throw. A target takes 7d8 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 6th.
