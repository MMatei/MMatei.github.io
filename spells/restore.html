Power Word: Restore

Level IX Animancy
Classes. Bard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Touch
Components. V, S
Material Components. —
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. You speak a word of power that instantly restores a target to its peak condition, setting its hp to max. If the creature is charmed, frightened, paralyzed, or stunned, the condition ends. If the creature is prone, it can use its reaction to stand up. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.
