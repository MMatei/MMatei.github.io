Power Word: Restore

Classes. Bard
School. Level 9 Animancy
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Touch
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous

You speak a word of power that instantly restores a target to its peak condition, setting its hp to max. If the creature is charmed, frightened, paralyzed, or stunned, the condition ends. If the creature is prone, it can use its reaction to stand up. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.
