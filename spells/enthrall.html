Enthrall

Level II Noumancy
Classes: Bard, Warlock
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 10 rounds
Target: Any creatures of your choice that can hear you, aren't immune to being charmed, and are within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Intuition. If you or your companions are fighting a target, you have disadvantage on the roll. On hit, the target has disadvantage on Perception checks made to perceive any creature other than you until the spell ends or until the target can no longer hear you. Furthermore, creatures other than you can attempt to hide from the target as if they were heavily obscured. The spell ends if you are incapacitated or can no longer speak.

You weave an enticing song that completely captures a creature's attention.
