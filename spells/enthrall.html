Enthrall

Level II Noumancy
Classes. Bard, Warlock
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 12 squares (18m)
Components. V, S
Duration. 10 rounds
Effect. Roll spellpower vs INT against creatures of your choice that you can see within range, that can hear you, and that aren't immune to being charmed. If you or your companions are fighting a creature, you have disadvantage on the roll. On hit, the target has disadvantage on Perception checks made to perceive any creature other than you until the spell ends or until the target can no longer hear you. The spell ends if you are incapacitated or can no longer speak.

You weave an enticing song that completely captures a creature's attention.
