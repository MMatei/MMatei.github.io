Humbling Smite

Level I Protomancy
Classes: Paladin
Casting Time: 1 quick action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: Self
Effect: Every time you hit with your weapon, roll spellpower vs Firmness to knock your target prone.

You pour sonic force into your weapon, so whenever you strike a powerful wave of sound slams into your enemy, forcing them to their knees.
