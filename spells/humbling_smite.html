Humbling Smite

Level I Protomancy
Classes. Paladin
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. Self
Components. V
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds

Effect. Every time you hit with your weapon, the target must make a Strength save or be knocked prone.

You pour sonic force into your weapon, so whenever you strike a powerful wave of sound slams into your enemy, forcing them to their knees.
