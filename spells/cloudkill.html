Cloudkill

Level V Conjuration
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Up to 10 minutes
Target: A 4-square radius area centered on a point within 24 squares (36m)
Effect: The target area fills with yellow-green fog and becomes heavily obscured. The fog spreads around corners. It lasts for the duration or until strong wind disperses the fog, ending the spell.
When a creature enters the spell's area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, roll spellpower vs Endurance. On hit, deal 5d8 poison damage. On miss, deal half damage. Creatures are affected even if they hold their breath or don't need to breathe.
The fog moves 2 squares away from you at the start of each of your turns, rolling along the surface of the ground. The vapors, being heavier than air, sink to the lowest level of the land, even pouring down openings.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 5th.
