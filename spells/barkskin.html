Barkskin

Level II Transmutation
Classes: Druid, Ranger
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour
Target: Self or a willing creature within 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: Until the spell ends, the target's Deflection cannot be less than 16, regardless of what kind of armor it is wearing.

Your skin turns to heavy oak bark that can absorb even hefty blows.
