Dancing Lights

Umbramancy Cantrip
Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Range: 24 squares (36m)
Effect: You create up to four torch-sized lights within range, making them appear as torches, lanterns, or glowing orbs that hover in the air for the duration. You can also combine the four lights into one glowing vaguely humanoid form of Medium size. Whichever form you choose, each light sheds dim light in a 2-square radius. As a bonus action on your turn, you can move the lights up to 12 squares to a new spot within range.
