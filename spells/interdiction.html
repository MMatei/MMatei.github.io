Interdiction

Level IV Abjuration
Classes: Druid
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour
Target: Self
Effect: A shimmering barrier extends out from you in a 2-square radius and moves with you, preventing any living creature (other than undead and constructs) from passing or reaching through. Such creatures can attack you with spells or ranged weapons, however.
If you move so that an affected creature is forced to pass through the barrier, the spell ends.
