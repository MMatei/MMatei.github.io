Sunburst

Level VIII Protomancy
Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Every creature in a 6-square radius centered on a point within 30 squares (45m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Endurance. Roll with advantage against undead and oozes. On hit, a creature takes 12d6 force damage and is blinded for 10 rounds. On miss, it only takes half damage.
You must repeat the roll for every target blinded by this spell at the end of each of their turns. On a miss, it is no longer blinded.
This spell dispels any darkness in its area that was created by a spell.

A vast column of otherworldly light shines on the target area, searing the flesh of all within.
