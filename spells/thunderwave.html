Thunderwave

Level I Protomancy
Classes. Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self (3-square cube)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Roll spellpower vs CON against every creature in a 3-square cube originating from you. On success, the target takes 2d8 sonic damage and is pushed 2 squares away from you. On failure, the creature takes half as much damage and isn’t pushed. In addition, unsecured objects that are completely within the area of effect are automatically pushed 2 squares away from you by the spell’s effect, and the spell emits a thunderous boom audible out to 60 squares (90m).
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

A wave of thunderous sound sweeps out from you, violently pushing enemies backwards and rattling them with deafening sonic damage.
