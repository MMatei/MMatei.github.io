Thunderwave

Level I Protomancy
Classes: Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: All creatures in a 3-square cube originating from you (except you)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Endurance. On hit, the target takes 2d8 sonic damage and is pushed 2 squares away from you. On miss, the target only takes half damage. In addition, unsecured objects that are completely within the area of effect are automatically pushed 2 squares away from you by the spell's effect, and the spell emits a thunderous boom audible out to 60 squares (90m).
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

A wave of thunderous sound sweeps out from you, violently pushing enemies backwards and rattling them with deafening sonic damage.
