Blinding Smite

Level III Protomancy
Classes. Paladin
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. Self
Components. V
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack during this spell's duration, your weapon flares with bright light, and the attack deals an extra 2d6 force damage to the target. Additionally, roll spellpower vs CON to blind the creature until the spell ends. You must successfully repeat this roll at the start of each of your turns in order to maintain the effect.

Your weapon is glowing with holy light that blinds enemies as effectively as it sears their sinning flesh.
