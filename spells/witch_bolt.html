Witch Bolt

Level I Protomancy
Classes. Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. V, S, M (a twig from a tree that has been struck by lightning)
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. Roll spellpower vs AC against a target you can see. On a hit, the target takes 1d12 electric damage, and on each of your turns for the duration, you can use your action to deal 1d12 electric damage to the target automatically. The spell ends if you use your action to do anything else. The spell also ends if the target is ever outside the spellâ€™s range or if it has total cover from you.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the initial damage increases by 1d12 for each slot level above 1st.

A beam of crackling, blue energy lances out towards your target, forming a sustained arc of lightning between you, that dances chaotically and pulses with energy.
