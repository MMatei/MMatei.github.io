Disguise Self

Level I Umbramancy Ritual
Classes. Bard, Cleric, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self
Components. V, S
Duration. 1 hour
Effect. You make yourself – including your clothing, armor, weapons, and other belongings on your person – look different until the spell ends or until you use your action to dismiss it. You can seem 30cm shorter or taller and can appear thin, fat, or in between. You can’t change your body type, so you must adopt a form that has the same basic arrangement of limbs. Otherwise, the extent of the illusion is up to you.
Roll spellpower to determine the quality of the illusion. Any creature with a Passive Perception equal or greater will disbelieve the illusion. A creature can also actively investigate the illusion, rolling Perception against the illusion's quality. If a creature physically inspects the illusion and its hand passes through, it automatically disbelieves it. If a creature discerns the illusion for what it is, then it becomes faint to the creature.
