Control Flames

Protomancy Cantrip
Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous or 1 hour (see below)
Target: A square within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Choose one of the following:
  • You instantaneously create a roaring flame in one square, provided that suitable fuel is present there (it doesn't have to be particularly flammable, but it needs to burn once set on fire). If a creature is present there, roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, deal 1d12 fire damage and the creature is set on fire.
  • You instantaneously extinguish all non-magical fires within a 1.5m cube.
  • You double or halve the area of bright light and dim light cast by a non-magical flame, change its color, or both. The change lasts for 1 hour.
  • You cause simple shapes (such as the vague form of a creature, an inanimate object, or a location) to appear within the flames and animate as you like. The shapes last for 1 hour.
If you cast this spell multiple times, you can have up to three of its non-instantaneous effects active at a time, and you can dismiss such an effect as an action.
