Guardian of Faith

Level IV Conjuration
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 8 hours
Target: An unoccupied square within 6 squares (9m)
Effect: Conjure a Large spectral guardian that hovers in and occupies the target space. Any creature hostile to you that moves to a space within 2 squares of the guardian for the first time on a turn is attacked by the guardian. Roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, deal 20 force damage. On miss, deal half damage. The guardian vanishes when it has dealt a total of 60 damage.

The guardian appears as a vague humanoid shape with a gleaming sword and a shield emblazoned with the symbol of your deity.
