Flame Strike

Classes: Cleric
School: Level 5 Evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 12 squares (18m)
Components: V, S, M (a pinch of sulphur)
Duration: Instantaneous

A vertical column of divine fire roars down from the heavens in a location you specify. Each creature in a 2-square radius, 12m-high cylinder centered on a point within range must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 4d6 fire damage and 4d6 radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the radiant damage (increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 5th.
