Flame Strike

Level V Protomancy
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Every creature in a 12m-high cylinder centered on a point within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, deal 4d6 fire damage and 4d6 force damage. On miss, deal half damage.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the force damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 5th.

A column of divine white fire blasts rains down on the sinners and reduces them to sanctified ash.
