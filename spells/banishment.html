Banishment

Level IV Abjuration
Classes: Cleric, Paladin, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: A creature you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Warding to banish the target to another plane of existence.
If the target is native to the plane of existence you're on, you banish the target to a harmless demiplane. While there, the target is incapacitated. The target remains there until the spel1 ends, at which point the target reappears in the space it left or in the nearest unoccupied space if that space is occupied.
If the target is native to a different plane of existence than the one you're on, the target is banished with a faint popping noise, returning to its home plane. If the spell ends before 10 rounds have passed, the target reappears in the space it left or in the nearest unoccupied space if that space is occupied. Otherwise, the target doesn't return.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spel1 using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 4th.

Begone, fiend!
