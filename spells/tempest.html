Tempest

Level IV Protomancy
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Range: 30 squares (45m)
Effect: A 4-square-radius sphere of whirling air springs into existence centered on a point you choose within range. The sphere remains for the spell's duration. Roll spellpower vs Firmness against every creature in the sphere when it appears or that ends its turn there. On hit, deal 2d6 crushing damage. The sphere's space is difficult terrain.
Until the spell ends, you can use a quick action on each of your turns to cause a bolt of lightning to leap from the center of the sphere toward one creature you choose within 12 squares of the center. Roll spellpower vs Deflection. You have advantage on the attack roll if the target is in the sphere. On hit, deal 4d6 electric damage.
Creatures within 6 squares of the sphere have disadvantage on Perception checks made to listen.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the electric damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 4th.
