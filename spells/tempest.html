Tempest

Classes: Sorcerer Wizard
School: Level 4 Evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 squares (45m)
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds

A 4-square-radius sphere of whirling air springs into existence centered on a point you choose within range. The sphere remains for the spell's duration. Each creature in the sphere when it appears or that ends its turn there must succeed on a Strength save or take 2d6 crushing damage. The sphere's space is difficult terrain.
Until the spell ends, you can use a bonus action on each of your turns to cause a bolt of lightning to leap from the center of the sphere toward one creature you choose within 12 squares of the center. Make a ranged spell attack. You have advantage on the attack roll if the target is in the sphere. On a hit, the target takes 4d6 lightning damage.
Creatures within 6 squares of the sphere have disadvantage on Perception checks made to listen.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the lightning damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 4th.
