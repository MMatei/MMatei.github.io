Dark Tentacles

Level IV Conjuration
Classes: Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Range: 18 squares (27m)
Effect: Squirming, ebony tentacles fill a 4-square area on ground that you can see within range. For the duration, these tentacles turn the ground in the area into difficult terrain.
When a creature enters the affected area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, the creature takes 3d6 crushing damage and is restrained by the tentacles until the spell ends. A creature that starts its turn in the area and is already restrained by the tentacles automatically takes 3d6 crushing damage.
The restrained creature or one that can touch that creature can use its action to make a Strength (Athletics) check vs 8 + spellpower. The restrained creature can also use its action to make an Escape Artist (must be proficient) check vs 6 + spellpower. On a success, the target is freed.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage (both initial and later) increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 4th.
