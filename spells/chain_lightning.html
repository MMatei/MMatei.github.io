Chain Lightning

Level VI Protomancy
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: A thing you can see within 30 squares (45m), plus a further three other things of your choice within 6 squares of the initial target; each target must be distinct
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, deal 10d8 electric damage. On miss, deal half damage.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, one additional bolt leaps from the first target to another target for each slot level above 6th.

As the bolt of electricity zaps the primary target, it immediately forks into up to three prongs of lightning that lash out at others unfortunate enough to be in range.
