Mass Healing Word

Level III Animancy
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 quick action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Up to six creatures you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: A target regains hit points equal to 1d6 + your spellpower. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the healing increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.

With a single word of prayer, you invigorate nearby allies and grant them succor.
