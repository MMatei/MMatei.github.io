Locate Creature

Classes. Bard Cleric Druid Paladin Ranger Wizard
School. Level 4 Divination
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self
Components. V, S, M (a bit of fur from a bloodhound)
Duration. 1 hour

Describe or name a creature that is familiar to you. You sense the direction to the creature’s location, as long as that creature is within 1km of you. If the creature is moving, you know the direction of its movement.
The spell can locate a specific creature known to you, or the nearest creature of a specific kind (such as a human or a unicorn), so long as you have seen such a creature up close – within 6 squares – at least once. If the creature you described or named is in a different form, such as being under the effects of a polymorph spell, this spell doesn’t locate the creature.
