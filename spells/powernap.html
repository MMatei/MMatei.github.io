Power Nap

Level III Noumancy
Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 10 minutes
Target: Up to six willing creatures, including self
Effect: The targets fall unconscious for the duration. The spell ends early for a target if it takes damage or if it is shaken or slapped awake. If a target remains unconscious for the full duration, it gains the benefit of a short rest, and it cannot be affected by this spell again until it finishes a long rest.
