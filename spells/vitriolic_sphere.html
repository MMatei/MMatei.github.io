Vitriolic Sphere

Classes: Sorcerer Wizard
School: Level 4 Evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 squares (45m)
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous

You point at a place within range, and a glowing ball of emerald acid streaks there and explodes in a 4-square radius. Each creature in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 10d4 acid damage and 5d4 acid damage at the end of its next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes half the initial damage and no damage at the end of its next turn.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the initial damage increases by 2d4 for each slot level above 4th.
