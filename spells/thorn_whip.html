Thorn Whip

Conjuration Cantrip
Classes. Druid
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Material Components. —
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Roll spellpower vs Firmness; on hit, the target takes 1d6 piercing damage and can be pulled up to 3 squares in a straight line towards you.
At Higher Levels. This spell's damage is 2d6 for a caster of level 5, 3d6 for a caster of level 11, 4d6 for a caster of level 17 and 5d6 for a caster of level 23.

The thorn in your hand expands rapidly towards your target and swiftly envelops it in a thorny lash made of soft wood that digs into its flesh. The lash tightens and shortens and drags the victim towards you, kicking and screaming.
