Thorn Whip

Classes. Druid
School. Transmutation Cantrip
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6` (9m)
Components. V, S, M (the stem of a plant with thorns)
Duration. Instantaneous

You create a long, vine-like whip covered in thorns that lashes out at your command toward a creature in range. Make a melee spell attack against the target. If the attack hits, the creature takes 1d6 piercing damage, and if the creature is Large or smaller, you pull the creature up to 2` closer to you.
