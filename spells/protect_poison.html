Protection from Poison

Level II Abjuration
Classes: Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 1 hour
Target: Self, or a willing creature within 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: If the target is poisoned, you neutralize the poison. If more than one poison afflicts it, you neutralize one poison that you know is present, or you neutralize one at random. For the duration, attacks that would poison the target are rolled with disadvantage, and it has resistance to poison damage.
