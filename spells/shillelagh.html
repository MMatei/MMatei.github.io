Shillelagh

Transmutation Cantrip
Classes: Druid
Casting Time: 1 quick action
Material Components: —
Duration: 10 rounds
Target: A wooden weapon you wield
Effect: You transform a wooden weapon you wield. For the duration, you can use your spellcasting ability (Wisdom for Druid) instead of Strength for the attack and damage rolls of melee attacks. The weapon becomes magical and its damage die becomes a d8. The spell ends if you cast it again or if you let go of the weapon.
At Higher Levels: At level 5, the weapon's damage die becomes a d10. At level 11, the weapon's damage die becomes a d12. At level 17, the weapon deals 2d8 damage. At level 23, the weapon deals 2d10 damage.

You imbue your wooden weapon with the vigor of nature. The wood grows and thickens in your hands, and it is now harder than steel and just as deadly. The wood contorts to offer you the best grip, and you feel as if the weapon is an extension of your own hand, to the point where you can handily rival master swordsmen in technique.
