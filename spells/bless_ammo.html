Bless Ammo

Transmutation Cantrip
Classes. Cleric, Druid, Warlock
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. Touch
Components. V, S
Duration. 10 rounds
Effect. You touch one to three pebbles and imbue them with magic. You or someone else can make a ranged spell attack with one of the pebbles by throwing it or hurling it with a sling. If thrown, it has a range of 12 squares (18m). If someone else attacks with the pebble, that attacker adds your spellcasting ability modifier to the attack roll. On a hit, the target takes crushing damage equal to 1d6 + your spellcasting ability modifier. Hit or miss, the spell then ends on the stone.
If you cast this spell again, the spell ends early on any pebbles still affected by it.
At Higher Levels. The pebble's damage becomes: 1d8 + spellcasting modifier at level 5, 1d10 + spellcasting modifier at level 11 and 1d12 + spellcasting modifier at level 17.
