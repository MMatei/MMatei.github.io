Bless

Level I Divination
Classes. Cleric, Paladin
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. V, S, M (a sprinkling of holy water)
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. You bless up to three creatures of your choice within range. Whenever a target makes an attack roll before the spell ends, the target can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to that attack roll.

May your arm be steady and your aim be true!
