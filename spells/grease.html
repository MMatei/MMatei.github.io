Grease

Level I Conjuration
Classes: Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 10 rounds
Target: A 2-square (3m) square centered on a point within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Slick grease covers the target ground turns it into difficult terrain for the duration. Grease is also easily flammable; if set on fire, all creatures in the area take 1d12 fire damage and are set on fire.
When the grease appears, each creature standing in its area must succeed on a Dexterity (Acrobatics) check vs 8 + spellpower or fall prone. A creature that enters the area or ends its turn there must also succeed on an Dexterity (Acrobatics) check vs 8 + spellpower or fall prone.
