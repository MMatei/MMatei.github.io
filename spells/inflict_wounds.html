Inflict Wounds

Level I Animancy
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: A creature within 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Deflection. On hit, the target takes 3d10 necrotic damage. If undead, the target is instead healed.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 1st.

You touch a foe and pure necrotic energy is unleashed from your palm, turning flesh into dead tissue.
