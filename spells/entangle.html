Entangle

Level I Conjuration
Classes: Druid
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: All creatures in a 4-square square within 18 squares (27m)
Effect: Grasping weeds and vines sprout from the ground in the target area, turning it into difficult terrain.
Roll spellpower vs Firmness. On hit, a target is restrained by the entangling plants until the spell ends. A creature restrained by the vines or one that can touch that creature can use its action to make a Strength (Athletics) check vs 8 + spellpower. The restrained creature can also use its action to make an Escape Artist (must be proficient) check vs 6 + spellpower. On a success, the target is freed.
