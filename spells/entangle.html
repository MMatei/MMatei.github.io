Entangle

Level I Conjuration
Classes. Druid
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 18 squares (27m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. Grasping weeds and vines sprout from the ground in a 4-square square starting from a point within range. For the duration, these plants turn the ground in the area into difficult terrain.
Roll spellpower vs STR against creatures in the area when you cast the spell. On hit, a creature is restrained by the entangling plants until the spell ends. AA creature restrained by the vines or one that can touch that creature can use its action to make a Force check vs 8 + spellpower. On a success, the target is freed.

A multitude of gnarly vines and stringy weeds magically sprout in the area. The ground itself is not affected, but all else above it is entangled by the mass of greneery unless if it is strong enough to struggle free.
