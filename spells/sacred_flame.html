Sacred Flame

Protomancy Cantrip
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: A creature you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Reflex; the target gains no benefit from cover. On hit, deal 1d8 force damage.
At Higher Levels: This spell's damage is 1d10 for a caster of level 5, 1d12 for a caster of level 11, 2d8 for a caster of level 17, and 2d10 for a caster of level 23.

You utter a short prayer and a searing white flame descends upon your target, ignoring all barriers to bring righteous punishment.
