Frostbite

Protomancy Cantrip
Classes. Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 12 squares (18m)
Material Components. —
Duration. Instantaneous

Effects. Roll spellpower vs Endurance against a target you can see within range. On success, it suffers 1d8 cold damage and disadvantage on the next weapon attack roll it makes before the end of its next turn.
At Higher Levels. This spell's damage is 2d8 for a caster of level 5, 3d8 for a caster of level 11, 4d8 for a caster of level 17 and 5d8 for a caster of level 23.

You concentrate briefly, and a frosty mist descends upon your target, numbing it, its skin going pale, its lips turning blue. They are chilled to the bone and barely able to swing that sword.
