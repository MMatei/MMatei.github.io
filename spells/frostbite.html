Frostbite

⚡ Protomancy Cantrip
Classes. Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 12 squares (18m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous

Effects. Choose a target you can see within range; it must make a Constitution save, or take 1d6 cold damage and suffer disadvantage on the next weapon attack roll it makes before the end of its next turn.
At Higher Levels. This spell's damage is 2d6 for a caster of level 5, 3d6 for a caster of level 11, 4d6 for a caster of level 17 and 5d6 for a caster of level 23.

You concentrate briefly, and a frosty mist descends upon your target, numbing it, its skin going pale, its lips turning blue. They are chilled to the bone and barely able to swing that sword.
