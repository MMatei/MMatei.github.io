Hold Person

Level II Noumancy
Classes. Bard, Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 12 squares (18m)
Components. V, S, M (straight piece of iron)
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. Roll spellpower vs CHA against a humanoid that you can see within range. On hit, the target is paralyzed for the duration. At the start of each of your turns, you must roll again to maintain the effect. On a failure, the spell ends on the target.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional humanoid for each slot level above 2nd. The humanoids must be within 6 squares of each other when you target them.

With but a simple mental command, you completely paralyze a humanoid in place.
