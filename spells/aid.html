Aid

Level II Abjuration
Classes: Cleric, Paladin
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 8 hours
Target: Up to three creatures of your choice within 6 squares (9m)
Effect: Each target’s maximum and current hp increase by 5 for the duration.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, a target’s hit points increase by an additional 5 for each slot level above 2nd.

Extraordinary bravery and resolve fills the hearts of your allies, spurring them onwards into battle in spite of pain and weariness.
