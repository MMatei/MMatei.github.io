Aid

Level II Abjuration
Classes. Cleric, Paladin
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. V, S, M (a tiny strip of white cloth)
Duration. 8 hours
Effect. Your spell bolsters your allies with toughness and resolve. Choose up to three creatures within range. Each target’s maximum and current hp increase by 5 for the duration.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, a target’s hit points increase by an additional 5 for each slot level above 2nd.

Uncharacteristic bravery fills the hearts of your allies, spurring them onwards into battle in spite of pain and weariness.
