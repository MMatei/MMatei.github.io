Lightning Lure

Classes. Sorcerer Warlock Wizard
School. Evocation Cantrip
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 3 squares (4.5m)
Components. V
Duration. Instantaneous

You create a lash of lightning energy that strikes at one creature of your choice that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pulled up to 2 squares in a straight line toward you and then take 1d8 lightning damage if it is within 1 square of you.
