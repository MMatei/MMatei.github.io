Lightning Lure

Protomancy Cantrip
Classes. Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 3 squares (4.5m)
Components. V
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. You create a lash of lightning energy that strikes at one creature of your choice that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Strength save or be pulled up to 2 squares in a straight line toward you and then take 1d10 lightning damage if it is within 1 square of you.
At Higher Levels. This spell's damage is 2d10 for a caster of level 5, 3d10 for a caster of level 11, 4d10 for a caster of level 17 and 5d10 for a caster of level 23.

Perfect for those who want to get up close and personal.
