Guiding Bolt

Level I Protomancy
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Components: V, S
Duration: 1 round
Target: Creature you can see within 24 squares (36m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Deflection. On hit, the target takes 4d6 force damage, and the next attack roll made against this target before the end of your next turn has advantage.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

You send a blinding streak of light against your foe, which both sears his flesh and lights it for all your allies to see. Should they strike before the light fades, they will enjoy an easy prey.
