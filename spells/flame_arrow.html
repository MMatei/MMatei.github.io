Flame Arrows

Level III Protomancy
Classes. Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Touch
Material Components. —
Duration. Concentration, up to 1 hour
Effect. You touch up to 12 arrows or bolts and imbue them with magical power. The ammunition will deal an additional 1d6 fire damage when it hits. The enchantment is lost whether the arrow hits or misses, and the arrow is consumed by flames.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the spell no longer requires concentration and the number of pieces of ammunition you can affect with this spell increases by three for each slot level above 3rd.
