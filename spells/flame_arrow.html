Flame Arrows

Classes. Druid Ranger Sorcerer Wizard
School. Level 3 Transmutation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Touch
Components. V, S
Duration. Concentration, up to 1 hour

You touch 12 arrows or bolts and imbue them with magical power. The ammunition will deal an additional 1d6 fire damage when it hits. The enchantment is lost whether the arrow hits or misses.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the spell no longer requires concentration and the number of pieces of ammunition you can affect with this spell increases by three for each slot level above 3rd.
