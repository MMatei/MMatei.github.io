True Strike

Divination Cantrip
Classes. Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. S
Duration. Up to 2 rounds
Effect. Select a visible target within range. On your next turn, you gain advantage on your attack rolls against the target, provided that this spell hasn't ended.

You gain supernatural insight and can see that small flaw in the opponents defense. He won't see it coming until it's too late.
