True Strike

Divination Cantrip
Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: A willing creature you touch
Effect: Once before the spell ends, the targeted creature can make an attack vs Deflection with advantage.

You gain supernatural insight and can see that small flaw in the opponents defense. A favorite among assassins and snipers.
