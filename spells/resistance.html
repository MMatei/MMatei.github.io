Resistance

Abjuration Cantrip
Classes: Cleric, Druid
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Up to 2 rounds
Target: Self, or a willing creature within 1 square
Effect: Once before the spell ends, the target can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to one defense of its choice. It can roll the die before or after the attacker makes its roll. The spell then ends.
At Higher Levels: At level 11, the die becomes a d6, at level 23, the die becomes a d8.

You place a reassuring hand on the shoulder of your ally and allow warmth to pass into him, strengthening his resolve for the trials ahead.
