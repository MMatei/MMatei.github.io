Blade Ward

Abjuration Cantrip
Classes. Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Touch
Material Components. —
Duration. 2 rounds
Effect. A willing creature you touch gains resistance against crushing, piercing, and slashing damage from metal objects.

You extend your hand and trace a sigil of warding in the air. A translucent shimmer envelops the target, and any metal object that passes through it is slowed a little.
