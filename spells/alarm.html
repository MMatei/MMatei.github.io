Level I Abjuration Ritual
Classes: Ranger, Wizard
Casting Time: 10 rounds (1 minute)
Material Components: —
Duration: 8 hours
Target: A door, a window, or an area no larger than a 4-square (6m) cube, within 6 squares (9m)
