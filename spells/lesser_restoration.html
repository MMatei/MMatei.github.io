Lesser Restoration

Level II Abjuration
Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Self, or one willing creature within 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: You touch the target and can end either one disease or one condition afflicting it. The condition can be blinded, deafened, paralyzed, or poisoned.
