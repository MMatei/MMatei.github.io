Hail of Thorns

Level I Conjuration
Classes. Ranger
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. Self
Components. V
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. The next time you hit a creature with a ranged weapon attack before this spell ends, the target and each creature within 1 square of it must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 1d10 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 1st.

As your enchanted arrow slams into the enemy, it splits into dozens of sharp wooden thorns that impale all who stand nearby.
