Hail of Thorns

Level I Conjuration
Classes: Ranger
Casting Time: 1 bonus action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: Self (all creatures within 1 square of a creature hit by one of your ranged weapon attacks)
Effect: The next time you hit a creature with a ranged weapon attack before this spell ends, roll spellpower vs Reflex against the target and each creature within 1 square of it. On hit, deal 1d10 piercing damage. On miss, deal half damage.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 1st.

As your enchanted arrow slams into the enemy, it splits into dozens of sharp wooden thorns that impale all who stand nearby.
