Hail of Thorns

Level I Conjuration
Classes. Ranger
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. Self
Components. V
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. The next time you hit a creature with a ranged weapon attack before this spell ends, roll spellpower vs DEX against the target and each creature within 1 square of it. A creature hit takes 1d10 piercing damage, or half as much damage on a failure.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 1st.

As your enchanted arrow slams into the enemy, it splits into dozens of sharp wooden thorns that impale all who stand nearby.
