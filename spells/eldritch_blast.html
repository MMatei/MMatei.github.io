Eldritch Blast

Protomancy Cantrip
Classes. Warlock
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 24 squares (36m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 force damage.
At Higher Levels. The spell creates more than one beam when you reach higher levels: two beams at 5th level, three beams at 11th level, four beams at 17th level and five beams at 23rd level. You can direct the beams at the same target or at different ones. Make a separate attack roll for each beam.

A beam of an unatural bright green color streaks towards your target, its pure magic tearing through armor and flesh as if through paper.
