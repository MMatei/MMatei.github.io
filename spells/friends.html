Friends

Noumancy Cantrip
Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: A creature of your choice that isn't hostile toward you
Effect: For the duration, you have advantage on all Charisma checks directed at the target. When the spell ends, the creature realizes that you have used magic to influence its mood and will react according to its personality and the nature of your interaction with it.
