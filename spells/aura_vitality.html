Aura of Vitality

Level III Animancy
Classes: Paladin
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: Self (6 square radius aura)
Effect: Healing energy radiates from you in an aura with a 6-square (9m) radius. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. You can use a quick action to cause one creature in the aura (including you) to regain 2d6 hit points.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the healing increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.
