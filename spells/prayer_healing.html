Prayer of Healing

Level II Animancy
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 10 minutes
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within 6 squares (9m)
Effect: Each target regains hit points equal to 2d8 + your spellpower. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
