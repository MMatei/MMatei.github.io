Spirit Guardians

Level III Conjuration
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Target: Self
Effect: You call forth spirits from your patron deity to protect you, whose appearance depends on your deity's preferences. The spirits will fly around you to a distance of 3 squares for the duration and attack your enemies.
