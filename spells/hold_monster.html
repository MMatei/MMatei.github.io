Hold Monster

Level V Noumancy
Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: A creature that you can see within 18 squares (27m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Will. On hit, the target is paralyzed for the duration. This spell has no effect on undead. At the end of each of the target's turns, you must roll again to maintain the effect.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 5th. The creatures must be within 6 squares of each other when you target them.

You override a creatures' will and hold it prisoner in its own body.
