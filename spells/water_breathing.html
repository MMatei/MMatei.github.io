Water Breathing

Level III Transmutation Ritual
Classes: Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 1 hour
Target: Up to ten willing creatures (including yourself) within 6 squares (9m)
Effect: This spell grants a target the ability to breathe underwater until the spell ends. Affected creatures also retain their normal mode of respiration.
