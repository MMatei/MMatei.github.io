Locate Animals or Plants

Level II Divination Ritual
Classes. Bard, Druid, Ranger
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self
Components. S
Duration. V, S, M (a bit of fur from a bloodhound)
Effect. Describe or name a specific kind of beast or plant. Concentrating on the voice of nature in your surroundings, you learn the direction and distance to the closest creature or plant of that kind within 5km, if any are present.
