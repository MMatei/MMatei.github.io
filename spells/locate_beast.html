Locate Animals or Plants

Level II Divination Ritual
Classes: Bard, Druid, Ranger
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Self (5km radius aura)
Effect: Describe or name a specific kind of beast or plant. Concentrating on the voice of nature in your surroundings, you learn the direction and distance to the closest creature or plant of that kind within 5km, if any are present. You also feel a rough estimate of the number of the specified beast/plant in the radius.
