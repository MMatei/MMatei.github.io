Lightning Arrow

Level III Transmutation
Classes. Ranger
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. Self
Material Components. —
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. The next time you make a ranged weapon attack during the spell’s duration, the weapon’s ammunition, or the weapon itself if it’s a thrown weapon, transforms into a bolt of lightning. Make the attack roll as normal. The target takes 4d8 electricity damage on a hit, or half as much damage on a miss, instead of the weapon’s normal damage.
Whether you hit or miss, roll spellpower vs Reflex against every creature within 2 squares (3m) of the target. On hit, deal 2d8 lightning damage or half as much damage on a miss.
The piece of ammunition or weapon then turns to fine white powder.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage for both effects of the spell increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 3rd.
