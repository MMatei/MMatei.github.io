Hex

Level I Noumancy
Classes. Warlock
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. 18 squares (27m)
Components. V, S, M (the petrified eye of a newt)
Duration. Concentration, up to 1 hour
Effect. You place a curse on a creature that you can see within range. Until the spell ends, you deal an extra 1d6 psychic damage to the target whenever you hit it with an attack. Also choose one ability when you cast the spell. The target has disadvantage on ability checks made with the chosen ability.
If the target drops to 0 hp before this spell ends, you can use a bonus action on any subsequent turn of yours to curse a new creature. A remove curse cast on the target ends this spell early.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd or 4th level, you can maintain your concentration on the spell for up to 8 hours. When you use a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you can maintain your concentration on the spell for up to 24 hours.

The target is plagued by a certainty of impeding doom at your hands.
