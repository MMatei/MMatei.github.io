Healing Word

Level I Animancy
Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid
Casting Time: 1 quick action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: A creature that you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Target regains hit points equal to 1d4 + your spellpower. If the creature is undead, it instead takes 1d4 + your spellpower force damage. This spell has no effect on constructs.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 1st.
