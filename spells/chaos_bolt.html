Chaos Bolt

Level I Divination
Classes: Sorcerer
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: A creature you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Deflection. On hit, deal 2d8 + 1d6 damage. Choose one of the d8s; the number rolled on that die determines the damage type as shown below.
d8Damage Type
1Acid
2Cold
3Fire
4Force
5Electric
6Poison
7Psychic
8Sonic

If you roll the same number on both d8s, the chaotic energy leaps from the target to a different creature of your choice within 6 squares (9m) of it. Roll spellpower vs Deflection against the new target, and make a new damage roll, which could cause the chaotic energy to leap again. However, a creature can only be affected once by a casting of this spell.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

You pull from the insane energies of the Far Realm and hurl an undulating, warbling mass of chaotic energy that shifts very rapidly in color.
