Reverse Gravity

Classes: Druid Sorcerer Wizard
School: Level 7 Transmutation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 20 squares (30m)
Components: V, S, M (a lodestone and iron filings)
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds

This spell reverses gravity in a 10-square-radius, 30m high cylinder centered on a point within range. All creatures and objects that arenâ€™t somehow anchored to the ground in the area fall upward and reach the top of the area when you cast this spell. If there's a fixed object in reach, a creature can make a Dexterity save to grab onto it, thus avoiding the fall. The creature is considered restrained while hanging in this manner.
If some solid object (such as a ceiling) is encountered in this fall, falling objects and creatures strike it just as they would during a normal downward fall. If an object or creature reaches the top of the area without striking anything, it remains there, oscillating slightly, for the duration.
At the end of the duration, affected objects and creatures fall back down.
