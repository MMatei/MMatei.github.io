Blindness/Deafness

Level II Animancy
Classes. Bard, Cleric, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. V
Duration. 10 rounds
Effect. Roll spellpower vs CON against one creature that you can see within range. On hit, the target is either blinded or deafened (your choice) for the duration. At the start of each of your turns, you must roll again to maintain the spell, or its effects ends
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.
