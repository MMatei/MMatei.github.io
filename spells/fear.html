Fear

Level III Umbramancy
Classes. Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self (6 square cone)
Material Components. —
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. You project a phantasmal image of a creature’s worst fears. Roll spellpower vs Awareness against every creature in a 6-square cone. On hit, a creature drops whatever it is holding and becomes frightened for the duration.
While frightened by this spell, a creature must take the Dash action and move away from you by the safest available route on each of its turns, unless there is nowhere to move. If the creature ends its turn in a location where it doesn’t have line of sight to you, you must roll spellpower vs Awareness in order to maintain the effect.
