Fear

Level III Noumancy
Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: Every creature in a 6-square (9m) cone
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Will. On hit, a target drops whatever it is holding and becomes frightened for the duration.
While frightened by this spell, a creature must take the Dash action and move away from you by the safest available route on each of its turns, unless there is nowhere to move. If the creature ends its turn in a location where you can no longer see it, then you must roll spellpower vs Will in order to maintain the effect.

You make a creature's worst nightmare surface in its mind's eye. Only the bravest souls can resist the urge to drop everything and flee.
