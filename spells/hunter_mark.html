Hunter's Mark

Level I Divination
Classes. Ranger
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. 18 squares (27m)
Components. V
Duration. Concentration, up to 1 hour
Effect. You choose a creature you can see within range and mystically mark it as your quarry. Until the spell ends, you deal an extra 1d6 damage to the target whenever you hit it with a weapon attack, and you have advantage on any Perception or Survival check you make to find it. If the target drops to 0 hp before this spell ends, you can use a bonus action on a subsequent turn of yours to mark a new creature.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd or 4th level, you can maintain your concentration on the spell for up to 8 hours. When you use a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you can maintain your concentration on the spell for up to 24 hours.
