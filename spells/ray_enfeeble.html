Ray of Enfeeblement

Level II Animancy
Classes: Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Up to 10 rounds
Target: A creature you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Endurance. On hit, the target deals only half damage with weapon attacks that use Strength until the spell ends. At the start of each of your turns, you must make the roll again in order to maintain the spell. On a failure, the spell ends.

A black beam from your finger towards your victim, making its muscles ache with fatigue. Its next swing will be decidedly less impressive!
