Gentle Repose

Level II Animancy Ritual
Classes: Cleric, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: 2 silver coins (worth at least 0.2 gg) placed on the corpse's eyes (or in the eye sockets) that must remain undisturbed for the duration
Duration: 10 days
Target: A corpse or other remains you touch
Effect: For the duration, the target is protected from decay and can’t become undead. The spell also effectively extends the time limit on raising the target from the dead, since days spent under the influence of this spell don’t count against the time limit of spells such as raise dead. The corpse will not attract necrophages, but if they stumble upon it they will still eat it.
