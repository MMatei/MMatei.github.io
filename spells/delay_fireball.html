Delayed Fireball

Level VII Protomancy
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: Every creature in a 4-square radius centered on a point within 30 squares (45m)
Effect: A beam of yellow light flashes from your pointing finger, then condenses to linger at a chosen point within range as a glowing bead for the duration.
When the spell ends, either because your concentration is broken or because you decide to end it, the bead blossoms with a low roar into an explosion of flame that spreads around corners. Roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, deal the accumulated fire damage. On miss, deal half damage.
The spell's base damage is 12d6. At the end of each of your turns, if the bead has not yet detonated, the damage increases by 1d6.
If the glowing bead is touched before the interval has expired, the creature touching it must roll Dexterity vs 8 + spellpower to prevent it from exploding immediately. If successful, the creature can throw the bead up to 8 squares. When it strikes a creature or a solid object, the spell ends, and the bead explodes.
