Shield of Faith

Level I Abjuration
Classes: Cleric, Paladin
Casting Time: 1 quick action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Target: Self, or a willing creature within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Grant the target a +2 bonus to Deflection for the duration.

A shimmering field appears and surrounds the target of your spell, slowing down weapons as they enter the field.
