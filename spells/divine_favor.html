Divine Favor

Level I Protomancy
Classes: Paladin
Casting Time: 1 bonus action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: Self
Effect: Until the spell ends, your weapon attacks deal an extra 1d4 force damage on a hit.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the extra damage increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 1st.

Your fervent prayer imbues your weapon with divine wrath.
