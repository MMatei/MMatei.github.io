Bane

Level I Noumancy
Classes. Bard, Cleric
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. V, S, M (a drop of blood)
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. Roll spellpower vs CHA against up to three creatures of your choice that you can see within range. On hit, every time the creature makes an attack roll before the spell ends, the target must roll a d4 and subtract the number rolled from the attack roll or saving throw.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.

This curse may be mild, but it can effectively ruin your lovely day.
