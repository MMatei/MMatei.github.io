Bane

Level I Noumancy
Classes: Bard, Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: Up to three creatures that you can see within 6 squares (9m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Warding. On hit, the target must subtract a d6 from every attack roll it makes.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.

This curse may seem mild, but it can effectively ruin an otherwise wonderful day.
