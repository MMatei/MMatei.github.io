Guidance

Divination Cantrip
Classes. Cleric, Druid
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Touch
Material Components. —
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effects. You touch one willing creature. Once before the spell ends, the target can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to one ability check of its choice. It can roll the die before or after making the ability check. The spell then ends.

You commune with your source of power and ask for guidance. A silver strand appears in your mind's eye, and you grasp it and attach it to an ally. They now briefly share your insight, and their task is clearer.
