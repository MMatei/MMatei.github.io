Staggering Smite

Classes. Paladin
School. Level 4 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. Self
Components. V
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds

The next time you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack during this spell’s duration, your weapon pierces both body and mind, and the attack deals an extra 4d6 psychic damage to the target. The target must make a Wisdom save or it has disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks, and can’t take reactions, until the end of its next turn.
