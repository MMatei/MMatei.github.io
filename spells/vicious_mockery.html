Vicious Mockery

Noumancy Cantrip
Classes: Bard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: A creature that can hear you (though it need not understand you) and that isn't immune to psychic damage within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Intuition. On hit, the target takes 1d6 psychic damage and has disadvantage on the next attack roll it makes before the end of its next turn.
At Higher Levels: This spell's damage is 2d6 for a caster of level 5, 3d6 for a caster of level 11, 4d6 for a caster of level 17 and 5d6 for a caster of level 23.

Speak your cleverest insult, apply just a hint of psychic magic, and the target is guaranteed to lose its wits, at least for the next attack.
