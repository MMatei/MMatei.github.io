Vicious Mockery

Noumancy Cantrip
Classes. Bard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 12 squares (18m)
Components. V
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Select a target in range that can hear you (though it need not understand you). It must succeed on an Intuition save or take 1d4 psychic damage and have disadvantage on the next attack roll it makes before the end of its next turn.
At Higher Levels. This spell's damage is 2d4 for a caster of level 5, 3d4 for a caster of level 11, 4d4 for a caster of level 17 and 5d4 for a caster of level 23.

Speak your cleverest insult, apply just a hint of psychic magic, and the target is guaranteed to lose its wits, at least for the next swing.
