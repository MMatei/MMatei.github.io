Web

Level II Conjuration
Classes. Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 12 squares (18m)
Components. V, S, M (a bit of spider web)
Duration. Concentration, up to 1 hour
Effect. You conjure a mass of thick, sticky webbing at a point of your choice within range. The webs fill a 4-square cube from that point for the duration. The webs are difficult terrain and lightly obscure their area.
If the webs arenâ€™t anchored between two solid masses (such as walls or trees) or layered across a floor, wall, or ceiling, the conjured web collapses on itself, and the spell ends at the start of your next turn. Webs layered over a flat surface have a depth of 1m.
Roll spellpower vs DEX against every creature that starts its turn in the webs or that enters them during its turn. On hit, the creature is restrained as long as it remains in the webs or until it breaks free. A creature restrained by the webs, or another creature that touches it, can use its action to make a Force check vs 8 + spellpower. If it succeeds, it is no longer restrained.
The webs are flammable and will burn away in 1 round, dealing 2d4 fire damage to any creature that starts its turn in the fire.
