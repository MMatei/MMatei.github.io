Thaumaturgy

Transmutation Cantrip
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Up to 10 rounds
Range: 6 squares (9m)
Effect:You create one of the following magical effects within range:
  • Your voice booms up to three times as loud as normal.
  • You grow your shadow up to twice its normal size.
  • You cause harmless tremors in the ground.
  • You create an instantaneous sound that originates from a point of your choice within range, such as a rumble of thunder, the cry of a raven, or ominous whispers.
  • You instantaneously cause an unlocked door or window to fly open or slam shut.
  • You alter the appearance of your eyes.
If you cast this spell multiple times, you can have up to three of its 10-round effects active at a time, and you can dismiss such an effect as an action.
