Power Word: Kill

Level IX Noumancy
Classes: Bard, Sorceror, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: A creature you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: If the target has 70hp or less, it dies. If the target has between 140 and 71 hp inclusive, then you must succeed on a spellpower vs Will roll. If the target has more than 140hp, the spell has no effect.

You command the target to cease its bodily functions immediately. Only the strongest can resist this compulsion.
