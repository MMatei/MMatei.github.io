Feather Fall

Level I Abjuration
Classes: Bards, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 reaction
Material Components: —
Duration: 10 rounds
Target: Up to five falling creatures within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: A target's rate of descent slows to 6m per round until the spell ends. If the creature lands before the spell ends, it takes no falling damage and can land on its feet, and the spell ends for that creature.
