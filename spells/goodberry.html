Goodberry

Level I Conjuration
Classes: Druid, Ranger
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 8 hours
Target: Self
Effect: Eight magical violet berries appear in your hand. A creature can use its action to eat one berry. Eating a berry restores 2 hp; eating all the berries provides enough water and nutrients to sustain a creature for a day. The berries begin to rot after about eight hours.
