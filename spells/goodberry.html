Goodberry

Level II Conjuration
Classes. Druid, Ranger
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Touch
Components. V, S, M (a sprig of mistletoe)
Duration. 4 hours
Effect. Up to ten magical violet berries appear in your hand. A creature can use its action to eat one berry. Eating a berry restores 2 hp; eating all the berries provides enough nourishment to sustain a creature for a day, including water. The berries begin to rot after four hours.
