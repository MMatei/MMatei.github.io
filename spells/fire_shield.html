Fire Shield

Level IV Protomancy
Classes: Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 10 minutes

Target: Self
Effect: Thin and wispy flames wreathe your body for the duration, shedding bright light in a 2-square radius and dim light for an additional 2 squares. You can end the spell early by using an action to dismiss it. The flames provide you with a warm shield or a chill shield (blue flames), as you choose. The warm shield grants you resistance to cold damage, and the chill shield grants you resistance to fire damage. In addition, whenever a creature within 1 square of you hits you with a melee attack, the shield erupts with flame. The attacker takes 2d8 fire damage from a warm shield, or 2d8 cold damage from a chill shield.
