Fire Shield

Classes. Wizard
School. Level 3 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self
Components. V, S, M (a firefly)
Duration. 10 minutes

Thin and wispy flames wreathe your body for the duration, shedding bright light in a 2-square radius and dim light for an additional 2 squares. You can end the spell early by using an action to dismiss it. The flames provide you with a warm shield or a chill shield, as you choose. The warm shield grants you resistance to cold damage, and the chill shield grants you resistance to fire damage. In addition, whenever a creature within 1 square of you hits you with a melee attack, the shield erupts with flame. The attacker takes 2d8 fire damage from a warm shield, or 2d8 cold damage from a cold shield.
