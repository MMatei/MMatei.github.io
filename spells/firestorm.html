Firestorm

Level VII Protomancy
Classes: Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: 30 squares (45m)
Effect: A storm made up of sheets of roaring flame appears in a location you choose within range. The area of the storm consists of up to ten 2-square cubes, which you can arrange as you wish. Each cube must have at least one face adjacent to the face of another cube. Roll spellpower vs Reflex against every creature in the area. On hit, deal 7d10 fire damage. On miss, deal half damage.
The fire damages objects in the area and flammable objects ignite if it isn't being worn or carried by a creature who spends its reaction to put out the fire. If you choose, plant life in the area is unaffected by this spell.
