Force Burst

⚡ Protomancy Cantrip
Classes. Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self
Components. V
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Each creature within 1 square of you takes 1d6 force damage.
At Higher Levels. This spell's damage is 2d6 for a caster of level 5, 3d6 for a caster of level 11, 4d6 for a caster of level 17 and 5d6 for a caster of level 23.

You gather up power nside you, then unleash it as a wave of pure force tearing through the flesh of those foolish enough to come near you.
