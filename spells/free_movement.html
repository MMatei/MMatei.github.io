Freedom of Movement

Level IV Abjuration
Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Ranger
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 1 hour
Target: Self, or a willing creature within 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: The target you touch is unaffected by difficult terrain, and spells and other magical effects can neither reduce the target’s speed nor cause the target to be paralyzed or restrained.
The target can also spend 1 square of movement to automatically escape from nonmagical restraints, such as manacles or a creature that has it grappled. Finally, being underwater imposes no penalties on the target’s movement or attacks.
