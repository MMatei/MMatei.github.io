Wall of Thorns

Level VI Conjuration
Classes: Druid
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Range: 24 squares (36m)
Effect: You create a wall of tough, pliable, tangled brush bristling with needle-sharp thorns. The wall appears within range on a solid surface and lasts for the duration. You choose to make the wall up to 12 squares long, 3m high, and 1.5m thick or a circle that has a 4-square diameter and is up to 6m high and 1.5m thick. The wall blocks line of sight.
When the wall appears, roll spellpower vs Reflex against every creature in the area. On hit, deal 7d8 piercing damage. On miss, deal half damage.
A creature can move through the wall, albeit slowly and painfully. For every 1 square a creature moves through the wall, it must spend 4 squares of movement. Furthermore, the first time a creature enters the wall on a turn or ends its turn there, roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, deal 7d8 piercing damage. On miss, deal half damage.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, both types of damage increase by 1d8 for each slot level above 6th.
