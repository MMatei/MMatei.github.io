Magic Missile

Level I Protomancy
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: One or more creatures you can see within 24 squares (36m)
Effect: You create three glowing darts of magical force. The darts strike simultaneously, but can be directed to several targets. A dart deals 1d4 + 1 force damage to its target.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the spell creates one more dart for each slot level above 1st.
