Create/Destroy Water

Level I Transmutation
Classes. Cleric, Druid
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. V, S, M (a drop of water if creating water, or a few grains of sand if destroying it)
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. You create up to 35 litres of clean water within range in an open container, or as rain in a 10m cube within range. Alernatively, destroy up to 35 litres of water within range, or fog within a 10m cube within range.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you create or destroy 35 additional litres of water, or the size of the cube increases by 2m, for each slot level above 1st.
