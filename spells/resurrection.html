Resurrection

Level VII Animancy
Classes: Bard, Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: A diamond worth at least 1,000 gg, which the spell consumes
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: A dead creature that has been dead for no more than a century, that didn't die of old age, and that isn't undead within 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: If its soul is free and willing, the target returns to life with all its hit points.
This spell neutralizes any poisons and cures normal diseases afflicting the creature when it died. It doesn't, however, remove magical diseases, curses, and the like; if such affects aren’t removed prior to casting the spell, they afflict the target on its return to life.
This spell closes all mortal wounds and restores any missing body parts.
Coming back from the dead is an ordeal. The target takes a -4 penalty to all rolls it makes and to all its defenses except Deflection. Every time the target finishes a long rest, the penalty is reduced by 1 until it disappears.
Casting this spell to restore life to a creature that has been dead for one year or longer taxes you greatly. Until you finish a long rest, you can’t cast spells again, and you take a -4 penalty to all rolls you make and to all defenses except Deflection.
