Burning Hands

Level I Protomancy
Classes. Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self (3-square cone)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Roll spellpower vs DEX against every creature in a 3-square cone. A creature takes 3d6 fire damage on hit, or half as much damage otherwise. The fire ignites any flammable objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

As you hold your hands with thumbs touching and fingers spread, a thin sheet of flames shoots forth from your outstretched fingertips, widening into a cone that scorches any creature unfortunate enough to stand in your path.
