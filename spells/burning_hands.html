Burning Hands

Classes. Sorcerer Wizard
School. Level 1 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self (3-square cone)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous

As you hold your hands with thumbs touching and fingers spread, a thin sheet of flames shoots forth from your outstretched fingertips. Each creature in a 3-square cone must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 3d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
