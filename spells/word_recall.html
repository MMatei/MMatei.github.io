Word of Recall

Level VI Conjuration
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Self and up to five willing creatures within 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: The targets instantly teleport to a previously designated sanctuary, appearing in the nearest unoccupied space to the spot you designated when you prepared your sanctuary (see below). If you cast this spell without first preparing a sanctuary, the spell has no effect.
You must designate a sanctuary by casting this spell within a location, such as a temple, dedicated to or strongly linked to your deity. If you attempt to cast the spell in this manner in an area that isn't dedicated to your deity, the spell has no effect.
