Animal Messenger

Level II Noumancy Ritual
Classes: Bard, Druid, Ranger
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 24 hours
Target: A Tiny beast you can see within 6 squares (9m)
Effect: By means of this spell, you use an animal such as a squirrel, a blue jay, or a bat to deliver a message. You specify a location, which you must have visited, and a recipient who matches a general description, such as "a man or woman dressed in the uniform of the town guard" or "a red-haired dwarf wearing a pointed hat". You also speak a message of up to twenty-five words. The target beast travels for the duration of the spell toward the specified location, covering about 80km per 24 hours for a flying messenger, or 40km for other animals.
When the messenger arrives, it delivers your message to the creature that you described, replicating the sound of your voice. The messenger speaks only to a creature matching the description you gave.
