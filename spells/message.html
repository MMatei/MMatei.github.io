Message

Noumancy Cantrip
Classes. Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 24 squares (36m)
Material Components. —
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. You point your finger toward a creature within range and whisper a message. The target (and only the target) hears the message and can reply in a whisper that only you can hear.
You can cast this spell through solid objects if you are familiar with the target and know it is beyond the barrier. Magical silence, 0.5m of stone, 1 cm of common metal, a thin sheet of lead, or 1m of wood blocks the spell.
