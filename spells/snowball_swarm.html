Snowball Swarm

Level II Protomancy
Classes. Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 18 squares (27m)
Components. V, S, M (a piece of ice or a small white rock chip)
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Roll spellpower vs DEX against every creature in a 1-square-radius sphere centered on a point you choose withing range. On hit, a creature takes 4d6 cold damage, or half as much damage on failure.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 2d6 for each slot level above 2nd.

A flurry of magic snowballs erupt from a point of your choosing, pelting all those unfortunate enough to stand nearby. Enhanced by magic, the snowballs cause severe frostburns on contact and can easily kill unprotected creatures.
