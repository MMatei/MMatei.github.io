Snowball Swarm

Classes. Sorcerer Wizard
School. Level 2 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 18 squares (27m)
Components. V, S, M (a piece of ice or a small white rock chip)
Duration. Instantaneous

A flurry of magic snowballs erupts from a point you choose within range. Each creature in a 1-square-radius sphere centered on that point must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 4d6 cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 2d6 for each slot level above 2nd.
