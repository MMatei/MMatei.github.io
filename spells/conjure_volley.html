Conjure Volley

Level V Conjuration
Classes: Ranger
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: A piece of ammunition or thrown weapon
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Every creature in a 8-square-radius, 6m-high cylinder centered on point within 30 squares (45m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Deflection. On hit deal 8d8 damage. On miss, deal half damage. The damage type is the same as that of the ammunition or weapon.

You fire a piece of nonmagical ammunition from a ranged weapon or throw a nonmagical weapon into the air and watch as hundreds of duplicates of the ammunition or weapon fall in a volley from above, skewering your helpless foes, only to then disappear.
