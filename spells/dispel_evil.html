Dispel Extraplanars

Level V Abjuration
Classes: Cleric, Paladin
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self
Components: V, S, M (powdered silver and iron)
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect: Shimmering energy surrounds and protects you from extraplanar creatures. For the duration, aberrations, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead have disadvantage on all attack rolls against you. You can end the spell early by using either of the following special functions:
  • Break Enchantment. As your action, you touch a creature you can reach that is charmed, frightened, or possessed by an extraplanar creature. The creature you touch is no longer charmed, frightened, or possessed by such creatures.
  • Dismissal. As your action, roll spellpower vs CHA against an extraplanar creature you can touch. On success, you banish the creature back to its home plane, if it isnâ€™t there already. Undead are sent to the Shadowfell, even if they did not originate there.
