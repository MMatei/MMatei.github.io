Tidal Wave

Level III Conjuration
Classes: Druid, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: 30 squares (45m)
Effect: You conjure up a wave of water 3m tall that crashes down on an area within range. The area can be up to 6 squares long and up to 2 squares wide. Roll spellpower vs Firmness against every creature in that area. On hit, a creature takes 4d8 crushing damage and is knocked prone. On miss, a creature takes only half damage. The water then spreads out across the ground in all directions, extinguishing unprotected flames in its area and within 6 squares of it.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 3rd.
