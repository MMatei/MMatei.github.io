Cloud of Daggers

Level II Conjuration
Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: All creatures in a 1-square cube, centered on a point within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Targets that start their turn in, or enter the affected area take 4d4 slashing damage.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 2d4 for each slot level above 2nd.

A myriad of translucent daggers fill a small area of your choice and begin to dance wildly, cutting any creature foolish enough to enter, with no room for escape for even the nimblest.
