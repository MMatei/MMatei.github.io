Divination

Level IV Divination Ritual
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 10 rounds
Material Components: Incense worth at least 25 gg, which the spell consumes
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Self
Effect: Your magic and an offering put you in contact with a Deity or a Deityâ€™s servants. You ask a single question concerning a specific goal, event, or activity to occur within 7 days. The GM offers a truthful reply to the best of his/her knowledge. The reply might be a short phrase, a cryptic rhyme, or an omen.
The spell cannot take into account unknown variables that might change the outcome. In particular, the GM has no control or foresight of the actions the players take; he can only speculate on the future, assuming a most likely chain of events.
If you cast this spell two or more times before finishing your next long rest, there is a cumulative 25 percent chance for each casting after the first that you get a random reading. The GM makes this roll in secret.
