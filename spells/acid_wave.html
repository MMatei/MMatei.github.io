Acid Wave

Level II Conjuration
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: All creatures in a line 6 squares long and 1 square wide, emanating from you in a direction you choose
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Deflection. On hit, deal 4d4 acid damage now and 4d4 acid damage at the end of the target's next turn. On miss, deal only half damage.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage (both initial and later) increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 2nd.

Unleash a bright yellow wave of acid towards your enemies and watch with joy as it bathes them in corrosive liquid.
Also known as Melf's Surprise for its inventor, the wizard Melf who loved to see smug smiles dissolve into acrid smoke.
