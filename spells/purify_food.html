Purify Food and Drink

Level I Transmutation Ritual
Classes: Cleric, Druid, Paladin
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: 1-square radius sphere within 6 squares (9m)
Effect: All nonmagical food and drink in the target area is purified and rendered free of poison, disease, and any ingredients harmful to the caster.
