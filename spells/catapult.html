Catapult

Level I Transmutation
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: An object that isn't being worn or carried, weighing 1 to 2 kg, within 30 squares (45m)
Effect: The object is flung in a straight line up to 18 squares (27m) in a direction you choose before falling to the ground, stopping early if it impacts against a solid surface. If the object would strike a creature, roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, the object strikes the target for 4d10 crushing damage and stops moving.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the maximum weight of objects that you can target with this spell increases by 2 kg, and the damage increases by 2d10, for each slot level above 1st.

Also known's as Galandor's Gamble, for the archmage who invented this spell and the all or nothing nature of this magick.
