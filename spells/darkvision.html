Darkvision

Classes. Druid Ranger Sorcerer Wizard
School. Level 2 Transmutation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Touch
Components. V, S, M (either a pinch of dried carrot or an agate)
Duration. 8 hours

You touch a willing creature to grant it the ability to see in the dark. For the duration, that creature has darkvision out to a range of 12 squares (18m).
