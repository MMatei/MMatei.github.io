Blade Barrier

Level VI Conjuration
Classes: Cleric
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: 12 squares (18m)
Effect: You summon myriad whirling, razor-sharp blades that form into a wall. The wall appears within range and lasts for the duration. You can make a straight wall up to 20 squares (30m) long, 6m high, and 1m thick, or a ringed wall up to 12 squares in diameter, 6m high, and 1m thick. The wall provides three-quarters cover to creatures behind it, and its space is difficult terrain.
When a creature enters the wall's area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, deal 6d10 slashing damage. On miss, deal half damage.
