Elemental Bane

Level IV Transmutation
Classes: Druid, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: A creature you can see within 18 squares (27m)
Effect: Choose of the following damage types: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder.
Roll spellpower vs Endurance. On hit, until the spell ends, the target loses its resistance to the selected damage type, and every time it suffers that type of damage, it takes an additional 2d6 damage.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 4th. The creatures must be within 6 squares of each other when you target them.
