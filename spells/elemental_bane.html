Elemental Bane

Classes. Druid Warlock Wizard
School. Level 4 Transmutation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 18 squares (27m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds

Choose one creature you can see within range, and choose one of the following damage types: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder.
The target must succeed on a Constitution save or be affected by the spell for its duration. Until the spell ends, the target loses its resistance to the selected damage type, and every time it suffers that type of damage, it takes an additional 2d6 damage.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 4th. The creatures must be within 6 squares of each other when you target them.
