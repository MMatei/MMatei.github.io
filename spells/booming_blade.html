Booming Blade

Protomancy Cantrip
Classes. Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Melee
Material Components. —
Duration. 10 rounds
Effect. As part of the action used to cast this spell, you must make a melee attack with a weapon against one creature within range, otherwise the spell fails. On a hit, the target suffers the attackâ€™s normal effects, and it becomes sheathed in sonic energy until the start of your next turn. If the target willingly moves before then, it immediately takes 1d8 sonic damage, and the spell ends.
At Higher Levels. At 5th level, the melee attack deals an extra 1d8 sonic damage to the target, and the damage the target takes for moving increases to 2d8. Both damage rolls increase by 1d8 at 11th level, at 17th level and at 23rd level.

Your weapon vibrates softly as you sheathe it in sonic energy. As you hit your enemy, the energy transfers to him, ready to be unleashed should he upset the delicate vibrations by running.
