Blight

Level IV Animancy
Classes. Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Material Components. —
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Roll spellpower vs Endurance. On hit, the target takes 8d10 necrotic damage, or half as much on a miss. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.
If you target a plant creature or a magical plant, you roll with advantage, and the spell deals maximum damage to it. If you target a nonmagical plant that isn't a creature, such as a tree or shrub, it simply withers and dies.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 4th.

Necrotic energy washes over your victim, draining moisture and life from it until only a dessicated husk remains.
