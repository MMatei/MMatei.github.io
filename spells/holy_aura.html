Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: Self (6-square radius aura)
Effect: Divine light washes out from you and coalesces in a soft radiance in a 6-square radius around you. Creatures of your choice in that radius when you cast this spell shed dim light in a 1-square radius and attacks against it are rolled with disadvantage. On hit, the creature is blinded until the spell ends.
