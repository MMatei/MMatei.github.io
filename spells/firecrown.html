Fire Crown

Level III Protomancy
Classes. Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self
Components. —
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Effect. You create six tiny flames that orbit around your head for the spell’s duration. When you cast the spell – and as a bonus action on each of your turns thereafter – you can expend one or two of the flames, sending them streaking toward a point or points you choose within 24 squares of you, where they suddenly expand into a roaring flame. Roll spellpower vs Reflex against every creature within 1 square of the point of the explosion. On hit, deal 2d6 fire damage, or half as much damage on a miss.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the number of orbiting flames created increases by two for each slot level above 3rd.
