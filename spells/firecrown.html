Fire Crown

Level III Protomancy
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Target: For each flame – all creatures within a 1-square sphere centered on a point within 24 squares
Effect: You create six tiny flames that orbit around your head for the spell’s duration. When you cast the spell – and as a quick action on each of your turns thereafter – you can expend one or two of the flames, sending them streaking toward a target area. Roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, deal 2d6 fire damage. On miss deal half damage. An easily flammable object hit by this spell ignites if it isn't being worn or carried by a creature who spends its reaction to put out the fire.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the number of orbiting flames created increases by two for each slot level above 3rd.
