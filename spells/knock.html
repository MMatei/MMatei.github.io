Knock

Level II Abjuration
Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: An object you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: A target that is held shut by a mundane lock or that is stuck or barred becomes unlocked, unstuck, or unbarred. If the object has multiple locks, only the strongest of them (GM's determination) is unlocked.
If you choose a target that is held shut with arcane lock, that spell is suppressed for 10 minutes, during which time the target can be opened and shut normally.
When you cast the spell, a loud knock, audible from as far away as 90m, emanates from the target object.
