Rope Trick

Level II Transmutation
Classes. Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Touch
Components. V, S, M (powdered corn extract and a twisted loop of parchment)
Duration. 1 hour
Effect. You touch a length of rope that is up to 20m long. One end of the rope then rises into the air until the whole rope hangs perpendicular to the ground. At the upper end of the rope, an invisible entrance opens to an extradimensional space that lasts until the spell ends.
The extradimensional space can be reached by climbing to the top of the rope. The space can hold as many as eight Medium or smaller creatures. The rope can be pulled into the space, making the rope disappear from view outside the space.
