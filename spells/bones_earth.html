Bones of the Earth

Level VI Transmutation
Classes: Druid
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: 24 squares (36m)
Effect: You cause up to six pillars of stone to burst from places on the ground that you can see within range. Each pillar is a cylinder that has a diameter of 1 square and a height of up to 9m. The ground where a pillar appears must be wide enough for its diameter, and you can target ground under a creature if that creature is Medium or smaller. Each pillar has Deflection 5 and 30 hp. When reduced to 0 hp, a pillar crumbles into rubble, which creates an area of difficult terrain with a 2-square radius. The rubble lasts until cleared.
If a pillar is created under a creature, roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, the creature is lifted by the pillar. A creature can choose to be hit.
If a pillar is prevented from reaching its full height because of a ceiling or other obstacle, a creature on the pillar takes 6d6 crushing damage and is restrained, pinched between the pillar and the obstacle. The restrained creature can use its action to make a Strength (Athletics) check vs 8 + spellpower. The restrained creature can also use its action to make an Escape Artist (must be proficient) check vs 6 + spellpower. On a success, the target is freed and it falls off the pillar.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, you can create two additional pillars for each slot level above 6th.
