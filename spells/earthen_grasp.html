Earthen Grasp

Level II Conjuration
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: An unoccupied square on the ground that you can see within 6 squares (9m)
Effect: A Medium hand made from compacted soil rises in the target square and reaches for one creature you can see within 1 square of it. Roll spellpower vs Firmness. On hit, the target takes 2d6 crushing damage and is restrained for the spellâ€™s duration. As an action, you can cause the hand to crush the restrained target, who takes 2d6 crushing damage.
The restrained creature or one that can touch that creature can use its action to make a Strength (Athletics) check vs 8 + spellpower. The restrained creature can also use its action to make an Escape Artist (must be proficient) check vs 6 + spellpower. On a success, the target is freed.
As an action, you can cause the hand to reach for a different creature or to move to a different unoccupied space within range. The hand releases a restrained target if you do either.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage (both initial and later) increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.
