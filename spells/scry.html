Scry

Level V Divination
Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 10 minutes
Material Components: A focus worth at least 1,000 gg, such as a crystal ball, a silver mirror, or a font filled with holy water
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Target: A creature you know of that is on the same plane of existence as you
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Warding, with the roll modified by the following factors:
KnowledgeRoll Bonus
Secondhand (you have heard of the target)-5
Firsthand (you have met the target)+0
Familiar (you know the target well)+5
ConnectionRoll Bonus
Likeness or picture+2
Posession or garment+4
Body part, lock of hair, or similar+10
On miss, the target isn’t affected, and you can’t use this spell against it again for 24 hours. On hit, the spell creates an invisible sensor within 2 squares of the target. You can see and hear through the sensor as if you were there. The sensor moves with the target, remaining within 2 squares of it for the duration. A creature that can see invisible objects sees the sensor as a luminous orb about the size of your fist.
Instead of targeting a creature, you can choose a location you have seen before as the target of this spell. When you do, the sensor appears at that location and doesn’t move.
