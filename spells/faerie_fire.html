Faerie Fire

Level I Umbramancy
Classes. Bard, Druid
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 12 squares (18m)
Components. V
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Effect. Each object in a 4-square cube within range is outlined in blue light. Any creature in the area when the spell is cast is also outlined in light if it fails a Dexterity saving throw. For the duration, objects and affected creatures shed dim light in a 2-square radius.
Any attack roll against an affected creature or object has advantage if the attacker can see it, and the affected creature or object can't benefit from being invisible.
