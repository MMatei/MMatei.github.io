Faerie Fire

Level I Umbramancy
Classes: Bard, Druid
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: Every object/creature in a 4-square cube within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Reflex. Objects are automatically affected. On hit, the target is clearly outlined in blue light for the duration, shedding dim light in a 2-square radius.
Any roll vs Deflection against an affected creature or object has advantage if the attacker can see it, and the affected creature or object can't benefit from being invisible.
