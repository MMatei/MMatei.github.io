Acid Splash

Conjuration Cantrip
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: One or two visible creatures within 1 square of each other, within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, deal 1d8 acid damage.
At Higher Levels: This spell's damage becomes 2d8 for a caster of level 5, 3d8 for a caster of level 11, 4d8 for a caster of level 17 and 5d8 for a caster of level 23.

You conjure a bubble of acid in your hand and then hurl it at your target. It impacts with a pleasing sizzle that turns flesh and metal into acrid smoke.
