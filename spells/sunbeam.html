Sunbeam

Level VI Protomancy
Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: Every creature in a 1-square wide, 12-square long line starting from you
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Endurance. Roll with advantage against undead or oozes. On hit, a target takes 6d8 radiant damage and is blinded until your next turn. On miss, it only takes half damage.
While the spell is in effect, you can use your action to repeat this attack. For the duration, a mote of brilliant sunlight shines in your hand. It sheds bright light in a 6-square radius and dim light for an additional 6 squares.

Concentrated sunlight, summoned from the Feywild, bursts from your hands, searing any in its path and rendering them blind.
