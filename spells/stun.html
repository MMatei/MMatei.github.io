Power Word Stun

Level VIII Noumancy
Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: A creature you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: If the target has 150 hp or fewer, it is stunned. Otherwise, the spell has no effect. You must roll spellpower vs Endurance at the end of each of the target's turns. On miss, this stunning effect ends.
