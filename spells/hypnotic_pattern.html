Hypnotic Pattern

Level III Umbramancy
Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Range: All creatures in a 6-square (9m) cube within 24 squares (36m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Intuition. On hit, a creature becomes incapacitated and has a speed of 0.
The spell ends for an affected creature if it takes any damage or if someone else uses an action to shake the creature out of its stupor.

You create a twisting pattern of colors that weaves through the air inside a small area, darting swiftly before the eyes of creature before disappearing.
