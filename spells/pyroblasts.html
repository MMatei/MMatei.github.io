Pyroblasts

Level IX Protomancy
Classes. Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 1 km
Material Components. A ruby worth at least 200 gg, which the spell consumes
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. You designate up to four different points in range. A huge fireball will explode violently at each designated point, affecting creatrues in an 8-square radius. Roll spellpower vs DEX against all those affected. On hit, a creature takes 20d6 fire and 20d6 crushing damage and is knocked prone to the nearest free space away from the explosion. On miss, takes only half damage but is still knocked prone. A creature in the area of more than one fiery burst is affected only once.
