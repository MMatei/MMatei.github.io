Pyroblasts

Classes. Sorcerer Wizard
School. Level 9 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 1 km
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous

You designate up to four different points in range. A huge fireball will crash from the sky on each designated point, exploding with tremendous force in an 8-square radius. Every creature caught in the explosion must make a Dexterity save or take 20d6 fire and 20d6 crushing damage, or half as much on a successful save. A creature in the area of more than one fiery burst is affected only once.
