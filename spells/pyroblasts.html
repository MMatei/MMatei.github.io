Pyroblasts

Level IX Protomancy
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: A ruby worth at least 200 gg, which the spell consumes
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: 1 km
Effect: You designate up to four different points in range. A huge fireball will explode violently at each designated point, affecting creatures in an 8-square radius. The sphere extends around corners. Roll spellpower vs Reflex against all those affected. On hit, a creature takes 20d6 fire and 20d6 crushing damage and is knocked prone to the nearest free space away from the explosion. On miss, deal only half damage but is still knocked prone. A creature in the area of more than one fiery burst is affected only once.
The spell damages objects in the area and ignites any flammable object.

Violent explosions set off, rattling the teeth in your mouth even though you stand quite the distance away.
