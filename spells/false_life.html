False Life

Level I Animancy
Classes. Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self
Components. V, S, M (a small amount of alcohol or distilled spirits)
Duration. 1 hour
Effect. Gain 5 + spellcasting modifier temporary hp for the duration.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you gain 5 + spellcasting modifier additional temporary hp for each slot level above 1st.

Drawing on an unstable stream of biotic energy, you infuse your body with energy beyond its limits, enabling you to withstand more punishment.
