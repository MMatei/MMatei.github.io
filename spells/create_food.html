Create Food and Water

Classes. Cleric Paladin
School. Level 3 Conjuration
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 6 squares (9m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous

You create 30kg of food and 30l of water on the ground or in containers within range, enough to sustain up to fifteen humanoids or five steeds for 24 hours. The food is a colorless gruel, bland but nourishing, and spoils if uneaten after 24 hours. Because of its lack of taste the food won't entice suspicious or hostile beasts.
