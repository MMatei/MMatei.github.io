Create Food and Water

Level III Conjuration
Classes: Cleric, Paladin
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: Salt worth 1 gg, which the spell consumes
Duration: Instantaneous
Range: 6 squares (9m)
Effect: You create 30kg of food and 30l of water on the ground or in containers within range, enough to sustain up to fifteen humanoids or five steeds for 24 hours. The food is a colorless gruel, bland but nourishing, and spoils if uneaten after 24 hours. Because of its lack of taste the food won't entice suspicious or hostile beasts.

Some religious orders make a point of only eating this bland gruel, which they see as the most holy of foods. Me, I prefer an old-fashioned pork chop, thank you very much.
