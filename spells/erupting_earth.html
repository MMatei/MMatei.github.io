Erupting Earth

Level III Transmutation
Classes. Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 24 squares (36m)
Components. V, S, M (a piece of obsidian)
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Choose a point you can see on the ground within range. Roll spellpower vs DEX against every creature in 4-square cube with its center within range. On hit, a creature takes 3d12 crushing damage and becomes prone. On failure, it takes half damage. Additionally, the ground in that area becomes difficult terrain until cleared away. Each square of the area requires at least 10 rounds to clear by hand.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d12 for each slot level above 2nd. The ground churns and erupts in a shower of deadly rocks.
