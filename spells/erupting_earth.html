Erupting Earth

Classes. Druid Sorcerer Wizard
School. Level 3 Transmutation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 24 squares (36m)
Components. V, S, M (a piece of obsidian)
Duration. Instantaneous

Choose a point you can see on the ground within range. A fountain of churned earth and stone erupts in a 4-square cube centered on that point. Each creature in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 3d12 bludgeoning damage and becomes prone on a failed save, or it takes half damage on a successful one. Additionally, the ground in that area becomes difficult terrain until cleared away. Each square of the area requires at least 10 rounds to clear by hand.
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d12 for each slot level above 2nd.
