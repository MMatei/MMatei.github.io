Spike Growth

Level II Transmutation
Classes: Druid, Ranger
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Range: 30 squares (45m)
Effect: The ground in a 4-square radius centered on a point within range twists and sprouts hard spikes and thorns. The area becomes difficult terrain for the duration. When a creature moves into or within the area, it takes 2d4 piercing damage for every square it travels.
The transformation of the ground is camouflaged to look like (mostly) harmless brambles, so that any creature that didn't see the area before the spell is cast must make a Perception check vs 8 + spellpower to recognize the terrain as hazardous before entering it.
