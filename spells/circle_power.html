Circle of Power

Level V Abjuration
Classes: Paladin
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Target: Self (6-square radius aura)
Effect: Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. For the duration, magical attacks and effects against friendly creatures in the area (including you) are rolled with disadvantage. Additionally, when a magical attack misses and it should deal half damage, it instead deals no damage.

Divine energy radiates from you, distorting and diffusing magical energy around you.
