Circle of Power

Classes. Paladin
School. Level 5 Abjuration
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self
Components. V
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Divine energy radiates from you, distorting and diffusing magical energy within 6 squares (9m) of you. Until the spell ends, the sphere moves with you, centered on you. For the duration, each friendly creature in the area (including you) has advantage on saves against spells and other magical effects. Additionally, when an affected creature succeeds on a save made against a spell or magical effect that allows it to make a save to take only half damage, it instead takes no damage.
