Heat Metal

Level II Protomancy
Classes: Bard, Druid
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: A manufactured metal object, such as a metal weapon or a suit of metal armor, that you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: You cause the target object to warm until it becomes red-hot. Any creature in physical contact with the object takes 2d8 fire damage when you cast the spell. Until the spell ends, you can use a quick action on each of your subsequent turns to cause this damage again.
For as long as a creature is holding or wearing the object and takes the damage from it, it has disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks. If the creature drops the object, the penalties cease.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
