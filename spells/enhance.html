Enhance Ability

Level II Transmutation
Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour
Target: Self, or a willing creature within 1 square
Effect: The target you touch gains one of the following enhancements until the spell ends:
  • Bear’s Endurance. The target has advantage on Constitution ability checks. It also gains 2d6 temporary hit points, which are lost when the spell ends.
  • Bull’s Strength. The target has advantage on Strength ability checks, and his or her carrying capacity doubles.
  • Cat’s Grace. The target has advantage on Dexterity ability checks. It also doesn’t take damage from falling 4 squares (6m) or less if it isn’t incapacitated.
  • Eagle’s Splendor. The target has advantage on Charisma ability checks.
  • Fox’s Cunning. The target has advantage on Intelligence ability checks.
  • Owl’s Wisdom. The target has advantage on Wisdom ability checks.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.
