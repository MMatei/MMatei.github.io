Telepathic Bond

Level V Divination Ritual
Classes: Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 1 hour
Target: Up to eight willing creatures within 6 squares (9m)
Effect: You psychically link each target to all the others for the duration. Mindless creatures aren't affected by this spell.
Until the spell ends, the targets can communicate telepathically through the bond whether or not they have a common language. The communication is possible over any distance, though it can't extend to other planes of existence.
