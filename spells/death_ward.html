Death Ward

Level IV Abjuration
Classes: Cleric, Paladin
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 8 hours
Range: Self, or a willing creature within 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: The touched creature gains a measure of protection from death. The first time the target would drop to 0 hp as a result of taking damage, the target instead drops to 1 hp, and the spell ends. If the spell is still in effect when the target is subjected to an effect that would kill it instantaneously without dealing damage, that effect is instead negated against the target, and the spells ends.
