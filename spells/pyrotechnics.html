Pyrotechnics

Level II Protomancy
Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: A fire that you can see within 12 squares (18m)
Effect: You can extinguish the chosen fire, and you create either fireworks or smoke in one of the squares of the fire.
  • Fireworks. The flame surges with a dazzling display of colors. Roll spellpower vs Endurance against every creature within 2 squares of the flame. On hit, a target becomes blinded until the end of your next turn.
  • Smoke. Thick black smoke spreads out from the chosen square in a 4-square radius, moving around corners. The area of the smoke is heavily obscured. The smoke persists for 10 rounds or until a strong wind disperses it.
