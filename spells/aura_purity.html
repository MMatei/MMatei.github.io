Aura of Purity

Classes. Paladin
School. Level 4 Abjuration
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self (6 square radius)
Components. V
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Purifying energy radiates from you in an aura with a 6-square radius. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. Each nonhostile creature in the aura (including you) can't become diseased, has resistance to poison damage, and has advantage on saving throws against effects that cause any of the following conditions: blinded, charmed, deafened, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, and stunned.
