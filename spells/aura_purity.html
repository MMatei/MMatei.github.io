Aura of Purity

Level IV Abjuration
Classes. Paladin
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self (6 square radius)
Material Components. —
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Effect. Purifying energy radiates from you in an aura with a 6-square radius. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. Each nonhostile creature in the aura (including you) can't become diseased, has resistance to poison damage, and attacks that would impose any of the following conditions: blinded, charmed, deafened, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, and stunned, are rolled with disadvantage.
