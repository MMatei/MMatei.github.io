Flaming Sphere

Level II Protomancy
Classes: Druid, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Range: 12 squares (18m)
Effect: A sphere of fire about 1m in diameter appears in an unoccupied space of your choice within range and lasts for the duration. Roll spellpower vs Reflex against any creature that ends its turn within 1 square of the sphere. On hit, deal 2d6 fire damage. On miss, deal half damage.
As a bonus action, you can move the sphere up to 6 squares. If you ram the sphere into a creature, roll the attack against that creature, and the sphere stops moving this turn.
When you move the sphere, you can direct it over barriers up to 1.5m tall and jump it across pits up to 2 squares wide. The sphere ignites flammable objects not being worn or carried, and it sheds bright light in a 4-square radius and dim light for an additional 4 squares.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.
