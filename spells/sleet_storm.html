Snow Storm

Level III Conjuration
Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Target: A 6m-tall cylinder with a 8-square radius centered on a point within 30 squares (45m)
Effect: Until the spell ends, a thick shower of snow falls incessantly in the target area, which becomes heavily obscured. Any exposed flames in the area are doused.
The ground in the area becomes covered in loose snow, making it difficult terrain. Furthermore, creatures entering the spell's area for the first time on a turn or starting their turn there must make a successful Dexterity (Acrobatics) vs 8 + spellpower roll or fall prone.
