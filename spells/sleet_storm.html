Sleet Storm

Classes. Druid Sorcerer Wizard
School. Level 3 Conjuration
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 30 squares (45m)
Components. V, S, M (a pinch of dust and a few drops of water)
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 rounds

Until the spell ends, freezing rain and sleet fall in a 6m-tall cylinder with a 8-square radius centered on a point you choose within range. The area is heavily obscured, and exposed flames in the area are doused.
The ground in the area is covered with slick ice, making it difficult terrain. When a creature enters the spell's area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, it must make a Dexterity save or it falls prone.
