Spider Climb

Level II Transmutation
Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: 1 hour
Target: Self, or a willing creature within 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: Until the spell ends, the target you touch gains the ability to move up, down, and across vertical surfaces and upside down along ceilings, while leaving its hands free. The target also gains a climbing speed equal to its walking speed.
