Cone of Cold

Level V Protomancy
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: Every creature in a 12-square (18m) cone
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Endurance. On hit, deal 8d8 cold damage. On miss, deal half damage. A creature killed by this spell becomes a frozen statue until it thaws.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 5th.
