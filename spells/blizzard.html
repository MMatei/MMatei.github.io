Blizzard

Level IV Conjuration
Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Instantaneous
Target: All creatures in a 6-square-radius, 12m-high cylinder centered on a point within 60 squares (90m)
Effect: Roll spellpower vs Reflex. On hit, a target takes 12d4 cold damage, and cannot take reactions or bonus actions until the end of its next turn. On miss, deal only half damage.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the cold damage increases by 2d4 for each slot level above 4th.

Large and sharp shards of hardened ice fall down in incessant waves that are very hard to avoid and can bring down even the hardiest of mountain folk. Furthermore, the intense cold the targets suffer makes them unable to quickly react to battlefield conditions for a short while afterwards.
