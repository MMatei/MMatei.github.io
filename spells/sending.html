Sending

Classes. Bard Cleric Wizard
School. Level 3 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Unlimited
Components. V, S, M (a copper wire)
Duration. 1 round

You send a short message of twenty-five words or less to a creature with whom you are familiar. The creature hears the message in its mind, recognizes you as the sender if it knows you, and can answer in a like manner immediately. The spell enables creatures with Intelligence scores of at least 1 to understand the meaning of your message.
