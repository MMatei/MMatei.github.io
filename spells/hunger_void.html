Hunger of the Void

Level III Conjuration
Classes: Warlock
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds
Range: 30 squares (45m)
Effect: You open a gateway to the dark between the stars, a region infested with unknown horrors. A 4-square-radius sphere of blackness and bitter cold appears, centered on a point with range and lasting for the duration. This void is filled with a cacophony of soft whispers and slurping noises that can be heard up to 6 squares away. No light, magical or otherwise, can illuminate the area, and creatures fully within the area are blinded.
The void creates a warp in the fabric of space, and the area is difficult terrain. Any creature that starts its turn in the area takes 2d6 cold damage. Furthermore, roll spellpower vs Reflex against any creature that ends its turn in the area. On hit, a target takes 2d6 acid damage as milky, otherworldly tentacles rub against it.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th or higher level, the acid damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.
