Sequester

Level VII Conjuration
Classes: Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: A powder composed of emerald, ruby, and sapphire dust worth at least 1,000 gg, which the spell consumes
Duration: Until dispelled
Target: A willing creature, or an object within 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: By means of this spell, the target is hidden away, safe from detection for the duration. When you cast the spell and touch the target, it becomes invisible and can’t be targeted by divination spells or perceived through scrying sensors of any kind.
If the target is a creature, it falls into a state of suspended animation. Time ceases to flow for it, and it doesn’t grow older.
You can set a condition for the spell to end early. The condition can be anything you choose, but it must occur or be visible within 2km of the target. Examples include after 1,000 years or when the tarrasque awakes. This spells also ends if the target takes any damage.
