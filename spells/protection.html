Protection from Extraplanars

Level I Abjuration
Classes: Cleric, Paladin, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: Pure salt, worth 25 gg, which the spell consumes
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Target: Self, or one willing creature within 1 square (1.5m)
Effect: Until the spell ends, the target you touch is protected against creatures not of the material plane: aberrations, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead.
The protection grants several benefits. Creatures of those types have disadvantage on attack rolls vs Deflection against the target. The target also cannot be charmed, frightened, or possessed by them. If the target is already charmed, frightened, or possessed by such a creature, then rolls to maintain the effect are made with disadvantage.
