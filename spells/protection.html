Protection from Good and Evil

Level I Abjuration
Classes. Cleric, Paladin, Warlock, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Touch
Components. V, S, M (holy water to ward against Evil, unholy water to ward against Good; consumed by the spell)
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Effect. Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch is protected against certain types of creatures: aberrations, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead.
