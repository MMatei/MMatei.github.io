Magic Weapon

Level II Transmutation
Classes: Paladin, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 quick action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour
Target: A weapon you touch
Effect: The target becomes a magic weapon (if it wasn't already), and it gains a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the bonus increases by +1 for every level above 3rd.

The weapon you touch takes on a subtle bluish hue that betrays its now magical nature.
