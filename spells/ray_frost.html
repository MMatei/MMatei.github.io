Ray of Frost

⚡ Protomancy Cantrip
Classes. Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 12 squares (18m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Instantaneous
Effect. Make a ranged spell attack against a visible target within range. On a hit, it takes 1d8 cold damage, and its speed is halved (to a minimum of 1) until the start of your next turn.
At Higher Levels. This spell's damage is 2d8 for a caster of level 5, 3d8 for a caster of level 11, 4d8 for a caster of level 17 and 5d8 for a caster of level 23.

A frigid beam of blue-white light streaks toward your target, numbing its legs and slowing its gait.
