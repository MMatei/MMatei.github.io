Fog Cloud

Level I Conjuration
Classes: Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour
Target: 4-square radius spehere centered on a point within 24 squares (36m)
Effect: You create a sphere of fog that spreads around corners. The target area is heavily obscured. It lasts for the duration or until a fairly strong wind disperses it.
At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the radius of the fog increases by 4 squares for each slot level above 1st.
