Fog Cloud

Classes. Druid Ranger Sorcerer Wizard
School. Level 1 Conjuration
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. 24 squares (36m)
Components. V, S
Duration. Concentration, up to 1 hour

You create a 4-square-radius sphere of fog centered on a point within range. The sphere spreads around corners, and its area is heavily obscured. It lasts for the duration or until a fairly strong wind disperses it.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the radius of the fog increases by 4 squares for each slot level above 1st.
