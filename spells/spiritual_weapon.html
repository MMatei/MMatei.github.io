Spiritual Weapon

Classes. Cleric
School. Level 2 Evocation
Casting Time. 1 bonus action
Range. 12 squares (18m)
Components. V, S
Duration. 10 rounds

You create a floating, spectral weapon within range that lasts for the duration or until you cast this spell again. When you cast the spell, you can make a melee spell attack against a creature within 1 square of the weapon. On a hit, the target takes force damage equal to 1d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier.
As a bonus action on your turn, you can move the weapon up to 4 squares and repeat the attack against a creature within 1 square of it. The weapon can take whatever form you choose. Clerics of deities who are associated with a particular weapon make this spellâ€™s effect resemble that weapon.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
