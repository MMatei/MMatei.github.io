Detect Extraplanars

Level I Divination
Classes: Cleric, Paladin
Casting Time: 1 action
Material Components: —
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Target: Self (12-square radius aura)
Effect: For the duration, you know if there is an aberration, celestial, elemental, fey, fiend, or undead within 12 squares of you, as well as where the creature is located. Similarly, you know if there is a place or object within 12 squares of you that has been hallowed, including the nature of the hallowing (good or evil).
The spell can penetrate most barriers, but it is blocked by 0.5m of stone, 1 cm of common metal, a thin sheet of lead, or 1m of wood or dirt.

Those creatures that do not belong on this plane have a peculiar presence that one can sense with the proper magic. This spell is similar to the divine sense of paladins.
