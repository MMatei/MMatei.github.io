Detect Evil and Good

Classes. Cleric Paladin
School. Level 1 Divination
Casting Time. 1 action
Range. Self
Components. V, S
Duration. Concentration, up to 10 minutes

For the duration, you know if there is an aberration, celestial, elemental, fey, fiend, or undead within 6 squares of you, as well as where the creature is located. Similarly, you know if there is a place or object within 6 squares of you that has been magically consecrated or desecrated.
The spell can penetrate most barriers, but it is blocked by 0.5m of stone, 1 cm of common metal, a thin sheet of lead, or 1m of wood or dirt.
